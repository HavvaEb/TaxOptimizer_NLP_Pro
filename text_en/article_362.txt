Tax on waste premises. - Notice Having regard to the Decree of 27 May 2004 introducing a tax on waste premises; Having regard to the Decree of 6 May 1999 on establishment, recovery and disputes relating to Walloon regional taxes; Article 4 (3) of the Decree of 27 May 2004 introducing a tax on waste premises provides for the principle of annual indexation of the tax on waste premises according to the evolution of the index of consumer prices between the months of June of the previous year (109,52) and of the current year (111,30). The rates for the taxable period 2022 are the following: - per are built area: 728,47 euro; - per are unbuilt area: 92,70 euro.
