Decision of the Walloon Government concerning the temporary prohibition of restrictions on access to public water distribution because of the urgent measures taken to limit the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19 The Walloon Government, having regard to Book II of the Environmental Code, which includes the Water Code, in particular Articles D.2, 28°, D.202 and Articles R.270bis-7 to R.270bis-13; having regard to the report of 19 March 2020 drawn up in accordance with Article 3 2° of the Decree of 11 April 2014 implementing the resolutions of the United Nations Women's Conference in Beijing in September 1995 and integrating the gender dimension into the whole of regional policies; having regard to the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, § 1; having regard to the urgency, which requires the opinion of the Council of State's legislation within a period of five days not to be delayed, taking into account in particular the periods for the purpose of interim billing of public water distribution, theIn so far as it is possible to obtain access to a meter subject to the social distancing rules imposed by the Federal Government, and without prejudice to the priority to be given to the actions for the continuity of public water distribution, the water distributors referred to in Article D.2, 28° of the Water Code shall not remove the water limiters installed pursuant to Article R.270bis-13 of the Water Code. Article 2. Water limiters referred to in Article R.270bis-13 of the Water Code may not be placed. Article 3. Interruptions in the public water distribution for domestic purposes as referred to in Article R.270bis-7 of the Water Code may be prohibited, subject to interruptions for technical or safety requirements. Article 4. This decision shall have effect on 1 April for a period of sixty days.
