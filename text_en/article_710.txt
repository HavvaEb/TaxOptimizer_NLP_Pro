Decision of the Minister-President of the Brussels Capital Region amending the Decision of 26 October 2020 laying down additional measures in addition to those laid down by the Minister-President of the Interior to restrict the dissemination of the Coronavirus COVID-19 The Minister-President of the Brussels Capital Region, Having regard to Article 166 (2) of the Constitution; Having regard to Article 48 of the Special Law of 12 January 1989 on the Brussels institutions, as amended by the Law of 6 January 2014; having regard to Article 11 of the Law of 5 August 1992 on the Police Office, as replaced by the Law of 7 December 1998; Having regard to Article 128 of the Province Law; having regard to the Ministerial Decision of 13 March 2020 on the Promulgation of the Federal Phase concerning the Coordination and Management of the Crisis Coronavirus COVID-19; having regard to the precautionary principle in the management of an international health crisis; having regard to the opinion of Celeval that the six guild rules to be applied in the various stages of the COVID-19 pandies to theWhereas the second paragraph of Article 27 (1) of the Ministerial Decision of 28 October 2020 laying down urgent measures to limit the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19 provides that "when the mayor or the governor is informed by the health entity concerned of a local increase in the epidemic in his territory, or when he establishes it, he must take additional measures by the situation." Whereas, on 28 October and 1 November 2020, the Minister of Interior has therefore adopted ministerial decisions to make all measures to the whole of the Belgian territory more stringent in order to make the contamination curve as quickly and radically as possible, in addition to the measures already adopted by the Federal Government, which have been adopted by the Minister of State in respect of its territory.Article 11 Article 16 of the same Decision is replaced by the following: "The measures imposed by this Decision shall apply until 13 December 2020. " Article 12 This Decision shall enter into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal. Article 13. In accordance with Article 14 of the coordinated laws of the Council of State, an action for annulment of this Decision may be brought before the Administrative Department of the Council of State for infringement of either substantial or penalty-free forms, excesses or reversal of power. The application for annulment must be submitted, under penalty of inadmissibility, within 60 days of publication in the Belgian Official Journal. The application may also be sent by post to the Registry of the Council of State, Rue de la Science, 33 in 1040 Brussels, or submitted in accordance with the electronic procedure using the identity card on the secure website of the State website http://eproadmin.raadvst-contetat.be.
