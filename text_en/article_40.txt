Art. 11. The fees referred to in this Decision shall not be granted or reimbursed: 1° in the event of bankruptcy, dissolution or voluntary or judicial liquidation of the non-profit-making association; 2° in the case of deliberate or non-profit-making provision by the association of incorrect or incomplete information, regardless of the effect of that information on the amount of the fees, without prejudice to the criminal prosecution of the persons who would have provided such information.
