Royal Decree declaring the collective agreement of 15 December 2020 concluded within the Joint Committee for Servers of International Trade, Transport and Logistics, to be binding on all of them on the basis of the COVID-19 crisis (1) FILIP, King of Belgians, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. Having regard to the law of 5 December 1968 concerning collective agreements and joint committees, in particular Article 28; having regard to the request of the Joint Committee for Servers of International Trade, Transport and Logistics; On the proposal of the Minister of Work, Hebben We decide and decide: Article 1. Generally binding the collective agreement of 15 December 2020, which is adopted as an annex to the Joint Committee for Servers of International Trade, Transport and Logistics, concerning measures following the COVID-19 crisis.Section 2. - Repayment by the Social Fund Article 3. The supplements referred to in Article 2 shall be paid no later than 1 March 2021 by the employer who may request reimbursement from the Social Fund. The reimbursement by the Social Fund shall be equal to EUR 5 per day temporary unemployment due to force majeure corona, within the available budget released from the Social Fund reserves and within the limits of Article 2 (1) paragraph. The application for reimbursement shall be addressed to the Social Fund which makes a form available to employers for this purpose. To be admissible, the application must be submitted to the Social Fund by 31 March 2021 at the latest. The refund shall be made during the second quarter 2021. This refund shall be within the purpose of the Social Fund (Article 3 of the Statute, Annex to the Collective Agreement of 7 September 2009 amending and coordinating the Statutes of the Social Fund for Security of Life, "Social Fund of the Joint Committee for Servants of International Trade, Transport and Logistics," Registration No 95868/CO/226).
