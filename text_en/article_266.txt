Decision of the Government of the French Community relating to special powers No 4 concerning support to the cultural sector in the context of the health crisis of the COVID-19 The Government of the French Community, having regard to the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis of the COVID-19, Article 1 (1) (d); having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 31 March 2020; having regard to the agreement of the Minister of Budget of 7 April 2020; having regard to the gender test carried out on 31 March 2020 pursuant to Article 4, paragraph 1, 1°, of the Decree of 7 January 2016 integrating the gender dimension into the whole of the policies of the French Community; having regard to the opinion 67.225/4 of the Council of State, given on 16 April 2020, pursuant to Article 84, paragraph 1, 3°, of the Laws of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; having regard to the urgency justified by the need to take action in support of theThe Council of State and the Constitutional Court in respect of the retroactive effect of the regulations in view of the exceptional circumstances which led to its adoption; on the proposal of the Minister of Culture; Decision: This Decision applying special powers to cultural matters, as referred to in Article 4, 1°, 3°, 4° and 4°, shall be subject to the conditions laid down in Article 5 of this Act.This Decision shall enter into force on the day of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
