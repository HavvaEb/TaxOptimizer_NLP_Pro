Having regard to the opinion of the Finance inspector given on 14 September 2021; having regard to the laws of the State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, paragraph 1; having regard to the urgency of the need; whereas in the context of the COVID-19 crisis, Article 3 of the Royal Decree of 21 September 2020, the Minister of Health, having regard to the law of 19 January 1961 authorizing persons who are legally not entitled to practice medicine, is granted an authorisation to carry out certain medical examinations, Article 2; having regard to the opinion of the Finance inspector given on 14 September 2021; having regard to the laws of the State, coordinated on 12 January 1973,This Decision shall enter into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal and shall enter into force on 1 October 2021. Brussels, 21 September 2020.
