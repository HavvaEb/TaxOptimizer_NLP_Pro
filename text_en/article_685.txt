Royal Decree to remediate the double use of Article 73 4quater/1 in the KB/WIB 92 FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greeting. Having regard to Article 205/5, § 5 of the Income Tax Code 1992 inserted by the Law of 25 December 2017; having regard to the Royal Decree of 16 November 1994 on administrative and budgetary control, Articles 5 and 14; whereas the provisions of this Decision may not have any direct or indirect financial impact; having regard to the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, Article 3, § 1; whereas the provisions of this Decision are not of a regulatory nature; whereas Article 3 of the Royal Decree of 20 December 2019 adjusting the KB/WIB 92 following the introduction of the group contribution and the limitation of interest, in the KB/WIB 92 is not adopted by Article 73 4quater/1; whereas Article 2 of the Royal Decree of 2 October 2019 to implement Article 205 (2) of the Royal Decree of 20 December 2019 on the Law of 20 December 1992 on the Law of 20 December 1992 on theMinister responsible for Finance is responsible for the implementation of this Decision.
