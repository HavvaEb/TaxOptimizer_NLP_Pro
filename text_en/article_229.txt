The Walloon Government's decision of 30 September 2021 extending to 30 September 2021 the provisions of the Walloon Government's decision of 30 June 2020 laying down various provisions on civil service in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic and the step-by-step reconstruction of activities The Walloon Government, having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 on institutional reform, Article 87 (3) replaced by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; having regard to the Walloon Government's decision of 18 December 2003 on the Walloon Civil Service Code, and in particular to Articles 361 and following, as amended; having regard to the Walloon Government's decision of 13 September 2019 on teleworking, as amended by the Walloon Government's decision of 31 January 2013, for its still applicable provisions; having regard to the Walloon Government's decision of 5 December 2019 concerning teleworking; having regard to the decision of 13 September 2019 determining the distribution of the ministerial powers and regulating the signature of its instruments, as amended;This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
