Decision of the Government of the French Community amending the Decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 concerning various provisions relating to civil service within the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic The Government of the French Community, Having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 on the reform of the institutions, Article 87 (3) replaced by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; Having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 22 July 1996 laying down the Staff Regulations of officials of the Services of the Government of the French Community; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 2 June 2004 concerning the leave and absence of staff of the Services of the Government of the French Community, the High Council for the Audiovisual Sector and the institutions of public utility under the Committee of Sector XVII; Having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 15 November 2017 concerning teleworking; having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of the Community, delivered on 25 June 2020;The Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 concerning various provisions relating to civil service in the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic shall be replaced by the following text: "Art. 2. § 1. Staff shall continue their activities in accordance with the provisions and conditions of this Article. § 2. The hierarchical superior of at least grade 12, or of at least grade 10, for services whose structure does not include a hierarchical superior of grade 12, designated either in a fixed context or in a higher post or ad interim, shall organise the planning of the performance of his team by telework and shall ensure a physical presence at the workplace of at least two days per week, with due regard to the necessary sanitary conditions. Where there is a need for continuity of the public service or where the nature of the work does not permit, the hierarchical superior may organize the planning of the work exclusively with physical presence at the workplace.Equal opportunities and supervision of WBE, Fr.
