Decision of the Government of the French Community of 30 July on the relations of the supervisory staff who are not responsible for training for social promotion in the framework of the COVID-19 health crisis The Government of the French Community, Having regard to the Decree of 16 April 1991 on the organisation of education for social promotion, Article 111; Having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 10 June 2021; Having regard to the finding of agreement of the Minister of Budget of 16 June 2021; Having regard to the gender test of 26 May 2021 adopted pursuant to Article 4, second paragraph 1°, of the Decree of 7 January 2016 integrating the gender dimension into the whole of the policies of the French Community; Having regard to the Protocol for trade negotiations in the Negotiating Committee of Sector IX, the Committee of the Provincial and Local Administrations - Section II and the Negotiating Committee for the Staff of Disabled Free Education in accordance with the procedures laid down in the Royal Decree of 28 September 1984 implementing the Law of 19 December 1974 concerning the relations between the Government and the trade unions of the workers who are on the basis of theThe Executive of the French Community of 27 December 1991 on the posts, assignments and relations of the members of the staff of the education for social promotion, for the calendar years 2020 and 2021, is affected by the COVID-19 crisis; whereas the number of teaching time pupils concerned will therefore be a parameter in the adaptation of the staff who are not responsible for training the calendar years 2022 and 2023 and consequently the staff who are not responsible for training the social promotion establishments of the calendar years 2022 and 2023 will be affected; whereas, in order to avoid this, it is necessary to offset the loss of the relations of the supervisory staff who are not responsible for training, by taking account of the teaching time-students of the last two calendar years preceding the crisis, namely 2018 and 2019; On the nomination of the Minister of Education for social promotion; Decision: Article 1.
