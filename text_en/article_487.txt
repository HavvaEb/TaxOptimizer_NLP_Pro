Decree approving the decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 11 of 14 May 2020 concerning support for the sector of higher education in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis The Parliament of the French Community has adopted and We, Government, confirm what follows: Sole Article. The decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 11 of 14 May 2020 concerning support for the sector of higher education in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis is confirmed in accordance with Article 4 of the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis in connection with the COVID-19 coronavirus.
