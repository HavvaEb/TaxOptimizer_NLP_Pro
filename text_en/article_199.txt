Decision of the Walloon Government on the basis of the special law of 8 August 1980 on the reform of the institutions, and in particular Article 87 (3) thereof, replaced by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; Having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 18 December 2003 on the Walloon Civil Service Code, and in particular Articles 361 and following thereof, as amended; Having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 7 April 2011 on teleworking, as amended by the decision of the Walloon Government of 31 January 2013, as regards its still applicable provisions; Having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 5 December 2019 on teleworking; Having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 13 September 2019 on the determination of the ministerial powers and on the signing of its instruments,This Decision shall apply to staff of the Walloon Government and the public institutions referred to in the Decree of 22 January 1998 laying down the Staff Regulations of certain institutions of public utility in the Walloon Region and "Wallonia-Bruxelles International."The provisions of this Decision shall continue to apply until 30 September 2020. They may be renewed by a decision of the Walloon Government. Article 8. This Decision shall enter into force on 1 July 2020. Names, 30 June 2020. For the Government, The Prime Minister, E.
