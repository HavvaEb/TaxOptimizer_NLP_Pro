having regard to the report of 13 October 2021 drawn up in accordance with Article 3 2° of the Decree of 11 April 2014 implementing the resolutions of the United Nations Women's Conference in Beijing in September 1995 and integrating the gender dimension into the whole of the regional policies; having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 14 October 2021; having regard to the assent of the Minister of Budget, given on 21 October 2021; having regard to the opinion of the State Council of 27 October 2021, given in accordance with Article 84 (1), first paragraph, 3nd, of the Laws of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; having regard to the urgency; having regard to the decision of the Ministerial Decision of 28 February 2020 on theFor the purposes of this Decision: 1° the Decree: the Decree of 11 March 2004 concerning regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises;
