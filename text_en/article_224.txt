The Royal Decree on the fourth allocation of the provisional appropriation entered in the programme 06-90-1 of the Law of 22 December 2020 laying down the general expenditure budget for the financial year 2021 and intended to cover the expenditure relating to the strengthening of the measures taken as well as the new initiatives relating to the fight against the COOVID-19 FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. Having regard to the Law of 22 December 2020 on the general expenditure budget for the financial year 2021, as amended by the Law of 2 April 2021 and the Law of 28 April 2021, Article 2.06.2; Having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 17 May 2021, is hereby registered to the programme 06-90-1 of the Law of 22 December 2020 concerning the general expenditure budget for the financial year 2021 with a provisional commitment credit and a provisional liquidation credit of 906,850,000 euro intended to cover the expenditure relating to the measures taken as well as to the new initiatives against the COVID 19 virus; whereas the Section 24 - FOD Social Security of the Law of 22 December 2020 on the general expenditure for the financial year 2021 does not apply to the budget for the budget for the budget for the year 2021;
