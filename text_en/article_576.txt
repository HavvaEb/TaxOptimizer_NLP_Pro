The Flemish Government's decision to grant aid to ornamental growers who have suffered a reduction in their turnover due to the operating restrictions imposed by the measures taken by the National Security Council from 12 March 2020 to COVID-19 Legal bases This decision is based on: - the Decree of 28 June 2013 on agricultural and fisheries policy, Article 9, paragraph 5°. Formal requirements The following formal requirements have been met: - The Flemish Minister, responsible for the budget, has given his agreement on 3 July 2020; - No opinion has been requested from the Council of State, applying Article 3, § 1 of the laws on the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973. There is an urgent necessity because the ornamental growers, as a result of the COVID-19 crisis and the compulsory closure of non-food shops, could no longer sell large parts of their crops. Most of the outlets abroad were also closed, while the Flemish ornamental sector is heavily export oriented. That situation has led to significant financial consequences and requires an immediate intervention to support the liquidity position of the companies involved and their legal framework.(a) 01.130 Growing of vegetables, melons and root and tuber vegetables;
