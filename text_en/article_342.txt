The Royal Decree concerning the composition and operation of the Covid-19 Commissioner's coordination cell and COVID-19 adjunct Commissioner FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greeting. Having regard to the Constitution, Article 37; having regard to the Royal Decree of 9 October 2020 appointing the COOVID-19 Commissioner and COOVID-19 adjunct Commissioner and determining their mandate; having regard to the opinions of the Finance Inspectors, given on 4 November 2020; having regard to the agreement of the Secretary of State for Budget, given on 9 November 2020; having regard to the agreement of the Minister of Civil Service, given on 10 November 2020; having regard to the laws of the State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, § 1; having regard to the urgent need; having regard to the Government's recent increase in the number of infections with the coronavirus COVID-19 adopted on 10 November 2020; having regard to the laws of the State,The provisions of Articles 10, 11, 13, 16 and 17 of the Royal Decree of 19 July 2001 concerning the interpretation of the policy bodies of the federal administrations and concerning the staff of the federal administrations designated to be part of a Cabinet of a Member of a Government or of a College of a Community or of a Region, and the provisions of Section 1 of Chapter XI of the Royal Decree of 19 November 1998 concerning the leave and absences granted to the staff of the government agencies, shall apply to the Director, the substantive members and the executive staff of the coordination cell. Art. 3. The Commissioner and the Deputy Commissioner may be assisted by a Scientific Committee. The members of this Committee shall be appointed by the Prime Minister and the Minister of Public Health on a proposal from the Commissioner and the Deputy Commissioner. The Prime Minister and the Minister of Health shall determine the remuneration to the members of the Scientific Committee. The Scientific Committee shall be composed in a multidisciplinary manner.
