Special Powers Decision No 2020/011 of the Brussels Capital Government authorising the Government to commit, settle and pay expenditure above the limit of budgetary appropriations or, in the absence of appropriations, to the amount fixed by a reasoned debate in the context of the health crisis COVID-19 The Brussels Capital Government; having regard to Article 39 of the Constitution; having regard to the special law of 16 January 1989 on the financing of the Communities and the Regions, as amended to date, and in particular to Title VII; having regard to the official decree of 23 February 2006 laying down the provisions applicable to the budget, accounts and control, as amended to date, and in particular Article 26 thereof; having regard to the decision of 17 December 2019 on the general budget of the Brussels Capital Region for the financial year 2020; having regard to the duty of 19 March 2020 to confer special powers on the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the health crisis COVID-19; having regard to the decision of the Brussels Government to the principle of 18 December 2019 to the principle of the Brussels Capital Region for the year 2020;Whereas, in these exceptional circumstances and in order to ensure the continuity of the public service and the execution of essential tasks, not only for the Region, but also for all socio-economic operators and for all Brusselsers, it is important that the Government should take into account budgetary considerations authorising the establishment, clearance and payment of expenditure beyond the limit of the budget appropriations or, in the absence of appropriations, to the extent of the amount fixed by the deliberations; whereas, in the light of this exceptional urgency during this period of COVID-19, it is absolutely necessary to derogate from Article 26 (2) of the Financial Regulation of 23 February 2006 on the basis of the provisions applicable to the budget, to the budget, to the extent of the amount fixed by the decision of the person concerned; whereas, in the light of this exceptional urgency during this period of COVID-19, theBy way of derogation from Article 26 (2) of the same decree, the Government may act independently in respect of the measures taken in the context of the COVID-19 crisis, and in the event of reasoned deliberation, it may decide to open the necessary appropriations and authorise the establishment, settlement and payment of expenditure above the limit of the budget appropriations or, in the absence of appropriations, the amount fixed by the debate. § 2. The application of Article 26 (2) of the abovementioned organic decree of 23 February 2006 shall be suspended for the period during which special powers are granted to the Brussels Capital Government referred to in Article 5 (1) of the Order of 19 March 2020 to grant special powers to the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the health crisis COVID-19. Art. 2. The measures taken in application of Article 1 of this Decision shall be confirmed in the context of the vote of the first adjustment of the Brussels Capital Region's general budget for the financial year 2020 or within the period referred to in Article 4 of the Brussels Convention.
