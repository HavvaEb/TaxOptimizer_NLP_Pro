The decree granting special powers to the Walloon Government to react to the second wave of the health crisis by COVID-19 (1) The Walloon Parliament has adopted and We, Walloon Government, confirm what follows: Article 1. § 1. In order to enable the Walloon Government to respond to the pandemic COVID-19, the Government may take all useful measures to prevent and deal with any situation which raises problems in the strict context of the pandemic COVID-19 and its consequences and which must be dealt with as a matter of urgency under severe danger. § 2. The decisions provided for in paragraph 1 may abolish, amend or replace, or even in matters expressly reserved to the decree by the Constitution. These decisions may, in particular, determine the administrative, civil and criminal penalties applicable to their infringement. The criminal penalties shall not contain penalties higher than those which are subject to the full, amended or replaced legislation applicable to the relevant infringements of this Decree. § 1.
