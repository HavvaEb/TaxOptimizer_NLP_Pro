The Government, acting in accordance with Article 39 of the Constitution. Article 2. § 1. In order to enable the Brussels Capital Region to react to the COVID-19 pandemic, the Government may take all appropriate measures, under threat of serious danger, to prevent and urgently address any situation within the strict framework of the COVID-19 pandemic and its consequences, in particular in the following areas: - the adaptation of legal texts on the deadlines laid down by the legislation of the Brussels Capital Region or those adopted pursuant to that legislation; - the adaptation of acts relating to areas in which the crisis has an impact and which are subject to regional powers;The decisions referred to in Article 2 shall be communicated to the President and the Registrar of Parliament before they are published in the Belgian Official Journal. Art. 4. The authorization granted to the Government by Article 2 of this decree shall apply two months from the date on which it enters into force. Art. 5. This decree shall enter into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal. Announce this decree, which shall be published in the Belgian Official Journal.
