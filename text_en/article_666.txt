Decision of the Flemish Government establishing the rules for granting a grant to the providers of mobility aids and to the undertakings commissioned by the providers of mobility aids to compensate for certain costs arising from the COVID-19 epidemic Legal basis This decision is based on: - the Decree of 18 May 2018 concerning the Flemish social protection, Article 107. Formal requirements The following formal requirements are fulfilled: - The Flemish Minister, responsible for the budget, has given his agreement on 22 April 2021. - No opinion has been requested from the Council of State, applying Article 3 (1) of the Laws on the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973. There is an urgent need because the corona epidemic requires the providers of mobility aids and the undertakings commissioned by the providers of mobility aids to invest in infrastructure and material to cope with the consequences of the COVID-19 epidemic.Invoicing of allowances for mobility aids. Art. 4. The subsidy referred to in Article 2 shall be indicated per provider acting as self-employed persons, in Article 105 (2) of the Decree of 18 May 2018 and per undertaking, set at 4.5% of the allowances invoiced before 1 February 2021 and approved by the healthcare greenhouses for all the following performances: 1° all performance in sales delivered during the period from 1 March 2020 to 31 December 2020; 2° all performance in maintenance and repair operations carried out during the period 1 March 2020 to 31 December 2020; 3° all performance months in rentals from March 2020 to December 2020. Art. 5. The grant referred to in Article 2 shall be charged to the budget 2020, Article GM0-AGHF2TK-WT: Operation and allowances - Mobility and aids.
