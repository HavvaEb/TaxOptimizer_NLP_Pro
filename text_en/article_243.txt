Decree approving the decision of the Government of the French Community of Special Powers No 8 of 7 May 2020 concerning the support to university hospitals in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis The Parliament of the French Community has adopted and We, Government, confirm the following: Sole Article. The decision of the Government of the French Community of Special Powers No 8 of 7 May 2020 concerning the support to university hospitals in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis, is confirmed in accordance with Article 4 of the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis related to the COVID-19 coronavirus. Announce this decree, recommend that it be published in the Belgian Official Journal. Brussels, 26 November 2020. The Prime Minister, P.-Y. JEHOLET The Vice-President and Minister of Budget, Officials, Equal Opportunities and Supervision of "Wallonia-Bruxelles Enseignement," DAERDEN The Vice-President and Minister of Child, Health, Culture, Media, Education and Higher Education, Education to the Minister of Youth, Education to the Minister of 25 November 2020.
