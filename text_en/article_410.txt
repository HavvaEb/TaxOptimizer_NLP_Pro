Environment Brussels. - Report. - Increase in the amount of the tax on waste incineration pursuant to Article 40 of the Ordinance of 14 June 2012 on waste Application of Article 40 of the Ordinance of 14 June 2012 on waste, from the tax year 2022, the amount of the tax on waste incineration shall be increased to EUR 15 per tonne of waste eligible for incineration. This amount shall be set at EUR 15 for the year 2022. With effect from 1 January 2022.
