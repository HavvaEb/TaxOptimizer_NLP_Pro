The Walloon Government's decision to relax the provisions on leave for imperative reasons in the context of the COVID 19 pandemic and the step-by-step reconstruction of activities The Walloon Government, having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 on reform of the institutions, Article 87 (3) replaced by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 18 December 2003 on the Walloon Civil Service Code, Articles 401 to 404, as amended; having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 13 September 2019 determining the distribution of ministerial powers and regulating the signature of its instruments; having regard to the Walloon Government's decision of 13 March 2020 laying down various provisions on civil service in the context of the coronavirus pandemic crisis and the gradual reconstruction of its activities,This decision will enter into force on 1 October 2020.
