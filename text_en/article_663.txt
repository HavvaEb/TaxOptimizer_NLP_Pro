The Minister of Finance, having regard to the Code of various rights and taxes, Article 201/9/1, first paragraph; having regard to the request for an opinion within 30 days, extended by 15 days, submitted to the Council of State on 24 June 2021, pursuant to Article 84 (1), first paragraph, 2°, of the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; whereas the opinion has not been communicated within that period; whereas the opinion has not been submitted to the Council of State within that period; whereas Article 84 (4), second paragraph, of the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; whereas it is a purely implementing act of an existing legislation and that decision has not been subject to any budgetary review, neither an opinion of the Finance inspector nor an agreement of the State Secretary of State for Budget is to be requested; Decision: Article 1. The leading official of the competent administrative office in charge of the collection and recovery of the accounts of the branches of the various branches of the Belgian authorities is to be established in Belgium in accordance with the conditions laid down in Article 201 of this Decision.
