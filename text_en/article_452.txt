Royal Decree amending Annex III to the Royal Decree 92 to the remuneration for student labour of FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greeting. Having regard to the Code of Income Tax 1992, Article 275, § 1; Having regard to the KB/WIB 92; Having regard to the opinion of the Finance Inspector given on 26 March 2021; having regard to the agreement of the State Secretary for Budget of 19 April 2021; having regard to the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, Article 3, § 1; Considering the urgent necessity; Whereas: - the above measure is taken to encourage students to go to work in the care sector and education for the purpose of calculating the annual quota of 475 hours not subject to social security contributions for the hours of study in April, May and June 2021;This Decision shall apply from 1 April 2021 and shall apply to remuneration paid or granted from that same date. Article 3. The Minister responsible for Finance shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision.
