Royal Decree amending the Royal Decree of 13 May 2020 introducing a grant "COVID-19" for the target group of the public centres for social welfare FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. Having regard to the Constitution, Article 108; Having regard to the Organic Law of 8 July 1976 on the public centres for social welfare, Articles 1 and 57, § 1 and 4; having regard to the Law of 22 May 2003 on the organisation of the budget and the comptability of the State, Article 122, paragraph 2; Having regard to the Law of 27 October 2020 amending Articles 5 § 2 and 6 of the Royal Decree of 13 May 2020 introducing a subsidy "COVID-19" for the target group of the public centres for social welfare (1); having regard to the Royal Decree of 13 May 2020 on the"COVID-19" for the target group of public social welfare centres shall be replaced by the following: " § 2. In order to justify the use of the allowance, the Centre shall: - by 28 February 2022 an electronic report containing the expenditure data for the years 2020 to 2021; - by 28 February 2023 an electronic report containing the expenditure data for the year 2022. These reports shall be transmitted through the "Unique Annual Report" web application. Original accounting documents shall remain available for the purpose of verifying the use of the allowance." Article 2. In Article 6 of the same Decision, the words "until 31 December 2021" shall be replaced by the words "until 31 March 2022." Article 3. The Minister responsible for Social Integration shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision.
