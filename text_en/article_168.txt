Royal Decree declaring the collective agreement of 23 November 2020 concluded in the Joint Subcommittee on Family and Older People's Assistance of the French Community, the Walloon Region and the German-speaking Community on temporary unemployment for the calculation of the final annual grant of 2020 in the context of the sanitary crisis COVID-19 in the services of family and elderly assistance of the French Community, the Walloon Region and the German-speaking Community (1) FILIP, King of Belgians, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. In accordance with the law of 5 December 1968 concerning collective agreements and joint committees, in particular Article 28; In view of the request of the Joint Subcommittee on Family and Older People's Assistance Services of the French Community, the Walloon Region and the German-speaking Community concerning the nomination of the Minister of Work, Hebben We decide and decide: Article 1.In the case of family and old-age assistance of the French Community, the Walloon Region and the German-speaking Community, recognized and subsidised by the Walloon Region. The term "workers" means the male and female workers. Article 2. Context This collective agreement is concluded in the context of the COVID-19 crisis. Art. 3. Subject-matter For the calculation of the end-of-year grant of 2020 (forfait part and variable part) as provided for in the collective agreement of 23 September 2019 granting a final premium to workers, the periods of temporary unemployment due to force majeure and temporary unemployment due to force majeure due to the coronavirus (corona unemployment) for the period 1 March 2020 to 31 December 2020 shall be included for the variable part calculated on the basis of force majeure and due to force majeure due to the coronavirus (corona unemployment) shall also apply to the amounts and further rules laid down in the collective agreements signed by Art. 4.
