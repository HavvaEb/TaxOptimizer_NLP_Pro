Decision of the Walloon Government concerning the granting of a specific concession to hotels and similar accommodations in the context of the crisis in relation to the Coronavirus COVID-19 The Walloon Government, having regard to the Decree of 11 March 2004 concerning regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises, and in particular Articles 10 and 19 thereof; having regard to the Decree of 11 March 2004 concerning regional incentives for large enterprises, Articles 7 and 15; having regard to the report of 24 February 2021 drawn up in accordance with Article 3 2° of the Decree of 11 April 2014 implementing the resolutions of the United Nations Conference on Women's Conference held in Beijing in September 1995 and integrating the gender dimension into the whole of regional policies; having regard to the opinion of the Finance Inspector, given on 23 February 2021; having regard to the assent of the Minister of Budget, given on 25 February 2021; having regard to the opinion of the Council of State, given on 5 March 2021, given to the first paragraph of Article 84, paragraph 3 of the Council on the conclusion of theThe Minister responsible for Economics, 2° Regulation (EC) No 651/2014, Commission Regulation (EC) No 651/2014 of 17 June 2014 declaring certain categories of aid compatible with the common market pursuant to Articles 107 and 108 of the Treaty;For the purposes of Article 10 of the Decree of 11 March 2004 concerning regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises and Article 7 of the Decree of 11 March 2004 concerning regional incentives for large enterprises. Article 3. The specific concession is granted under the conditions laid down in point 22 of the Temporary Framework. Article 4. The Minister shall, in accordance with the detailed rules laid down by him, grant a specific concession to the undertaking: 1° the main activity of which is covered by NACE-BEL code 55.100; 2° which is the owner of an establishment unit within the meaning of Article I.2., 16°, of Book I of the Code of Economic Law prior to the publication of this Decision; 3° which is subject to the laws governing its activity, as well as to the tax laws and regulations governing matters or which is liable to take into account the time limits laid down by the competent administration;The amount of the specific concession shall be calculated by the Administration in accordance with the first paragraph of Article 4 on the basis of the information communicated to it through the authentic sources or through the undertaking. The Administration may also use the databases constituting authentic sources to obtain all the information necessary for the examination of the file. Article 7. The decision on the admissibility, payment, control and recovery of the specific allowances shall be taken by each staff member of Level A as defined in the Walloon Government's decision of 18 December 2003 on the Walloon Code of Civil Service, designated by the Director-General of the Administration.SUS The Minister for Civil Service, Computer Science, Administrative Simplification, in charge of Child Benefit, Tourism, Heritage and Road Safety, V.
