Royal Decree amending the Royal Decree of 28 November 1969 implementing the Law of 27 June 1969 revising the Decree Law of 28 December 1944 concerning the social security of workers extending the period of validity of the meal cheques, ecocheques, gift cheques and sports/culture cheques resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. Having regard to the Law of 27 June 1969 revising the Decree Law of 28 December 1944 concerning the social security of workers, Article 14 (2); Having regard to the Law of 29 June 1981 concerning the general principles of social security for workers, Article 23, paragraph 2, as amended by the Royal Decree of 24 July 2008; Having regard to the Royal Decree of 28 November 1969 implementing the Law of 27 June 1969 on the revision of the Decree Law of 28 December 1944 on social security of workers, Article 19, paragraph 14, inserted by the Royal Decree of 13 July 2007 and amended by the Royal Decree of 3 July 2018,On the basis of Article 8 of the Law of 15 December 2013 laying down various provisions on administrative simplification, this Decision is not exempt from a regulatory impact analysis based on the urgency of the socio-economic risk posed by the pandemic of coronavirus COVID-19; having regard to the request for urgent treatment, justified by the situation with regard to the coronavirus Covid-19, which does not allow the opinion of the Council of State's legislation to be delayed within 30 days, taking into account in particular the need to take without delay the necessary measures to mitigate the socio-economic consequences of the emergency measures taken to limit the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19 by the World Health Organization (WHO) on the basis of the outbreak of SARS-CoV-2 virus in China in December 2019, the rapid spread of which was not foreseen throughout the world; having regard to the declaration of the international emergency for public health (PHEIC) by the World Health Organisation (WHO) on 30 January 2020; having regard to the designation of the coronavirus COVID-19 as a pest in China on 11 March 2020;Article 1, § 2, 14°, of the Royal Decree of 28 November 1969 implementing the Law of 27 June 1969 revising the Decree Law of 28 December 1944 concerning the social security of workers, inserted by the Royal Decree of 13 July 2007 and amended by the Royal Decree of 3 July 2018, shall be supplemented by a member, stating: "The validity of the Gift Cheques, which expire in March, April, May and June 2020, shall be extended by 6 months. The Minister for Social Affairs may, by a ministerial decision, after consulting the National Labour Council, fix a period of more than 6 months." Article 19a, § 2, 4°, of the same Decision, as last inserted by the Royal Decree of 29 June 2014, be supplemented by a member: "In derogation from the first paragraph, the period of validity of the electronic meal cheques, which expire in March, May and June 2020, shall be extended by six months.
