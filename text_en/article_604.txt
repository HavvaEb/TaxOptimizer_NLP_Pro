The Royal Decree on the ninth allocation of the provisional appropriation entered in the programme 06-90-1 of the Law of 22 December 2020 laying down the general expenditure budget for the financial year 2021 and intended to cover the expenditure relating to the strengthening of the measures taken as well as the new initiatives relating to the fight against the COOVID-19 FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. Having regard to the Law of 22 December 2020 on the general expenditure budget for the financial year 2021, as amended by the Law of 2 April 2021, the Law of 28 April 2021 and the Law of 27 June 2021, Article 2.06.2; having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 18 November 2021, is hereby registered with the programme 06-90-1 of the Law of 22 December 2020 concerning the general expenditure budget for the financial year 2021 a provisional commitment credit and a provisional clearance credit of 906.850,000 euro, intended to cover the expenditure relating to the measures taken as well as the new initiatives against the COVID 19 virus.Implementation of this Decision.
