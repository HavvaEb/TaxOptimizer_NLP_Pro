The Royal Decree amending Table g, first section, heading i, of the Annex to Royal Decree No 41 of 30 January 1987, fixing the amount of proportional tax fines in respect of value added tax REPORT TO THE KING Sire, The draft royal decree which we submit for signature to Your Majesty, aims to amend Table G, first section, heading I, 2.A) of Royal Decree No 41 of 30 January 1987, fixing the amount of proportional tax fines in respect of value added tax, the penalty rate shown therein is temporarily reduced from 15 pc to 10 pc. This reduction in the rate of fine is based on harmonisation with the penalty rate applicable to all or part of non-payment or non-time payment of the tax, the rate of which is shown by declarations other than those referred to in Article 53, § 1, first paragraph, 2° of the VAT Code (periodic VAT or quarterly returns).of the amount of proportional tax fines in respect of value added tax FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greeting.
