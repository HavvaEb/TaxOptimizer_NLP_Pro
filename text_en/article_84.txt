Decree approving the decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 15 of 20 May 2020 on aid to recognized youth services No 15 of 20 May 2020 on aid to recognised youth services in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis is hereby approved in accordance with Article 4 of the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the COVID-19 corona virus.
