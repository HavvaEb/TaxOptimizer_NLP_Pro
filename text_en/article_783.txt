Royal Decree amending Annex III of the KB/WIB 92 in respect of statutory benefits for temporary unemployment FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greetings. Having regard to the Code of Income Tax 1992, Article 275, § 1; Having regard to the KB/WIB 92; Having regard to the opinion of the Finance inspector given on 4 June 2021; Having regard to the State Secretary for Budget's agreement of 15 June 2021; having regard to the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, Article 3, § 1; having regard to the urgent need; whereas the reduction of the percentage of company tax on statutory benefits for temporary unemployment of 26,75 pct. to 15 pct. applies only to the benefits until 30 June 2021; - that this percentage of 26,75 pct. was taken into account for a limited number of days of temporary unemployment on a monthly basis;with the implementation of this Decision. Data in Brussels, 20 June 2021. FILIP Van Koningswege: The Minister of Finance, V. VAN PETEGHEM
