Decision of the Government of the French Community concerning various provisions relating to civil service within the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic The Government of the French Community, having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 on the reform of the institutions, Article 87 (3) is replaced by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; Having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 22 July 1996 laying down the statute of officials of the Services of the Government of the French Community; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 2 June 2004 concerning the leave and absence of staff of the Services of the Government of the French Community, the High Council for the Audiovisual Sector and the institutions of public utility attached to the Committee of Sector XVII; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 15 November 2017 concerning teleworking; having regard to the opinions of the Inspector of Finance, delivered on 22 and 29 April 2020; having regard to the agreement of the Minister of the Budget of 7 May 2020;This decision shall apply to the Government services of the French Community and to the institutions of public utility within the Sector Committee XVII. Article 2. § 1. Staff shall continue their activities in accordance with the detailed rules of this Article. § 2. The hierarchical superior of at least grade 12, or of at least grade 10 for services of which the structure does not include a hierarchical superior of grade 12, which is either permanently designated in a higher post or ad interim, organises the planning of his team by systematically using teleworking, except in cases where the continuity of the public service is imperative or if the nature of the services does not permit it. Staff members who are required to go to work for the needs of the service shall have to comply with the guidelines on social distance.The French Community of 2 June 2004 concerning the leave and absences of staff of the Services of the Government of the French Community, the High Council for the Audiovisual Sector and the institutions of public utility belonging to the Committee of Sector XVII; 2° leave for imperative family reasons, without limitation in time, by way of derogation from Article 16 of the Decision of the Government of the French Community of 2 June 2004 concerning the leave and absences of staff of the Services of the Government of the French Community, the High Council for the Audiovisual Sector and the institutions of public utility under the Committee of Sector XVII.
