Law amending the Code of Income Tax 1992 to limit the reference COainfera2bendinferb emissions (1) FILIP, King of Belgium, To all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greetings. The Chamber of Representatives has adopted and We confirm, which is as follows: Article 1. This law regulates a matter as referred to in Article 74 of the Constitution Art. 2. In Article 36, § 2 of the Income Tax Code 1992, replaced by the Law of 28 December 2011 and last amended by the Law of 25 December 2017, itself amended by the Law of 2 May 2019, the fifth paragraph is replaced as follows: "The King determines annually the reference CO 2 emissions in function of the average CO 2 emissions over a period of 12 consecutive months ending on 30 September of the year preceding the taxable period compared to the average CO 2 emissions of the reference year 2011 according to the modalities that He lays down. The average CO 2 emissions are calculated on the basis of the CO 2 emissions of the vehicles referred to in Article 65. The reference CO 2 emissions may never be higher than the reference emissions from the previous year.
