Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 55 derogating from various provisions relating to supplementary secondary vocational education, nursing department in the context of the health crisis COVID-19 The Government of the French Community, having regard to the Decree of 14 November 2020 granting special powers to the Government of the French Community of special powers No 35 of 18 June 2020 derogating from various provisions relating to supplementary secondary vocational education, nursing department in the context of the health crisis COVID-19; Having regard to the Decree of 11 May 2017 concerning the fourth degree of secondary vocational training, nursing department in the light of the urgent need to enable the French Community to react as soon as possible to the measures taken in the context of the second wave of the distribution of COVID-19:These specific conditions for the organisation of lessons and schools in particular relate to the number of days present at school and to extra-mural activities; whereas, in view of the difficulties encountered in the organisation of training courses following the second wave of the dissemination of COOVID-19, the minimum number of clinical hours of lessons should be reduced to 2,300, i.e. the minimum laid down by Directive 2013/55/EU of the European Parliament and of the Council of the European Union of 20 November 2013, and the difference of 166 hours should be converted into theoretical training devoted to seminars and reflection on professional practice; whereas it is necessary to derogate from the distribution of the volumes per hour of clinical and theoretical education per year of study in the light of the present health context, and, instead, to achieve the attainment of those volumes at the end of the training as a whole, for students registered in the first year of EPSC, nursing department, in 2020-2021, in the light of the present health context, to respect this distribution per year, and to achieve the degree of hospital and psychia at the end of the period in question of the year in question;From the supplementary secondary vocational education division of nursing, the clinical apprenticeship of 2,760 teaching times (2,300 hours) for pupils enrolled in 2020-2021 in the first, second, third and third years of secondary vocational education, nursing department, and this until they obtain the certification of hospital nurse - guidance of mental health care and psychiatry, provided that the pupils concerned at the end of the school year 2020-2021 are at least successful for the first year. § 2. by way of derogation from Article 3, § 5 of the same decree includes the theoretical training of 2,648 teaching hours (2, 206 hours) for pupils enrolled in the first, second, third and third years of supplementary secondary vocational education, nursing department, and this until they obtain the certificate of hospital nurse - orientation of mental health care and psychiatry, on condition that the pupils concerned at the end of the school year 2020-2021 are at least successful for the first year.Art. 5 By way of derogation from Articles 18, 19, 20, 21, 22, 23, 24 and 25 of the same decree, the distribution of teaching times of clinical education shall not apply until the end of the study cycle of the pupils entered in the supplementary secondary vocational education department in 2020-2021, provided that they have at least been successful for the first year of learning at the end of the school year 2020-2021. At the end of his training the pupil must have completed all these teaching times of clinical education.
