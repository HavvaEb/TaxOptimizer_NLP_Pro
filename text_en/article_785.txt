Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 15 concerning support to recognized services for youth in the context of the health crisis COVID-19 The Government of the French Community, Having regard to the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis in connection with the Covid-19, Article 1, (d); Having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 10 April 2020; Having regard to the agreement of the Minister of Budget, given on 7 April 2020; Having regard to the opinion of the Community Council of Prevention, Assistance to Youth, given on 17 April 2020; Having regard to the opinion of the Council of State, given on 23 January 2016 concerning the integration of the gender dimension into the whole of the policies of the French Community; Having regard to the opinion of the Council of 5 April 2020, adopted on 17 April 2020;Whereas the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis in connection with the Covid-19 empowers the Government to take all necessary measures to take account of the effects of the containment measures on the activities of operators and organisers of events of which the Community participates in its financing; whereas the pandemic in connection with the dissemination of the Covid-19-coronavirus and the resulting large-scale health crisis, in particular in Belgium; whereas the absence of any personnel on account of the illness in the services of young people receiving assistance is a matter for the benefit of those services; whereas those services are subject to the conditions laid down in Article 1 of Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71;(a) the decision of the Government of the French Community of 5 December 2018 concerning the special conditions for the recognition and subsidisation of the general resident services;2020.
