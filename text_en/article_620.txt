Decision of the Government of the French Community amending the Decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 laying down various provisions relating to civil service within the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic The Government of the French Community, having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 concerning institutional reforms, Article 87 (3) replaced by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; having regard to the decree of the Government of the French Community of 22 July 1996 concerning the Staff Regulations of the Staff of the Government of the French Community; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 2 June 2004 concerning the leave and absence of the staff of the French authorities of the French Community, of the Conseil Supérieur de l'Audiovisuel and of the institutions of the Sector Committee XVII; having regard to the decision of the Government of 15 November 2017 concerning telework; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 laying down various provisions concerning civil service within the framework of the COVID-19mmie, adopted by the French Government of 2 January 2020;Member of the decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 laying down various provisions on civil service in the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic, replaced by the decisions of the Government of the French Community of 2 July 2020, 3 September 2020, 29 October 2020 and 10 December 2020, the words "28 February 2021' shall be replaced by the words "30 April 2021'.
