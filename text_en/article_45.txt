Ministerial decision to extend for one month the measures relating to the crisis COVID-19 concerning the temporary prohibition of restrictions on access to the public water distribution The Minister of the Environment, Nature, Forests, Rural Affairs and Animal Welfare, having regard to Book II of the Environmental Code, comprising the Water Code, Secret part, Articles D.202 and D.232; having regard to Book II of the Environmental Code, comprising the Water Code, Regulational Part, Article R.270bis-13; having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 22 April 2020 concerning the temporary prohibition of restrictions on access to the public water distribution due to urgent measures to limit the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19, taking into account the duration of the health crisis related to COVID-19 and the maintenance of various measures to limit the spread of the virus to the population, which require permanent and adequate access to water;
