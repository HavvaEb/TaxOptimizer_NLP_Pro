The Royal Decree laying down, in accordance with Article 194septies/1, § 5 of the Code of Income Tax 1992, the model of the declaration and the detailed rules referred to in that Article, FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. Having regard to the Constitution, Article 108; Having regard to the Code of Income Tax 1992, Article 194septies/1, § 5, inserted by the Law of 23 June 2020 and amended by the Law of 15 July 2020; having regard to the Royal Decree of 16 November 1994 concerning administrative and budgetary control, Articles 5 and 14; whereas it is a purely implementing act of an existing legislation and this decision in itself does not have any new budgetary impact; having regard to the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, § 1;The tax year to which the declaration relates is the tax year for which the exemption is claimed, the taxable person shall, in this declaration, fill in the boxes "Definition of the temporarily exempted reserve" and "Detail of the headings in the box "Amountable reserved profit" that were reduced when the exempt reserve was created or increased when the exempt reserve was taken back." In the case where the tax year concerned is the tax year in which the total amount exempted during the previous tax year is reintegrated into the taxable reserved profit, the taxable person shall, in this declaration, fill in the boxes "Detail of the items in the taxable reserved profit" which were reduced or increased at the time the tax year 2019 or 2020 when the tax return of the exempt reserve was made" and "Data relating to the re-integration of the exempt reserve in the taxable reserved profit" in Art. 2. In the event of the declaration relating to a taxable period linked to the tax year 2019 or 2020 when the tax year of the tax year in force, at the time of this Royal Decree, is already in force, this declaration shall be sent to the tax year on 30 November 2020 which the competent tax form is attached to the tax year in question.
