The Royal Decree establishing the model of the tax return of non-residents (corporations, associations, etc.) for the tax year 2021 FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greeting. In the light of the Income Tax Code 1992, Article 307, as amended by the Law of 22 July 1993, by the Royal Decree of 20 December 1996, by the Laws of 15 March 1999, 10 August 2001, 8 June 2009, 29 March 2012, 27 December 2012, 30 July 2013, 25 April 2014, by the Programme Law of 10 August 2015, and by the Laws of 26 December 2015, 1 July 2016, 7 February 2018, 30 July 2018, 17 March 2019, 2 May 2019, 20 December 2020 and 26 January 2021, Article 307a (3) of the Income Tax Code 1992, inserted by the Law of 17 June 2013, stipulates that taxable persons are to be subject to the tax of non-residents, etc.
