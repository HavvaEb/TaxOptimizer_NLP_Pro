The Minister of Health and Social Affairs, having regard to the Constitution, Article 108; having regard to Royal Decree No 10 of 19 April 2020 for the award and the rules for the distribution and settlement of an advance to general hospitals under the Coronavirus COVID-19 epidemic, inserted by Royal Decree No 35 of 24 June 2020; having regard to the Royal Decree of 30 October 2020 laying down detailed rules for the granting of an exceptional federal financial aid to hospitals under the Coronavirus COVID-19 epidemic, Article 3, § 2; having regard to the opinions of the Federal Council for Hospital Facilities, given on 24 September 2020 and 10 December 2020,On the proposal of the Minister for Social Affairs and Public Health, Decision : Article 1. The flat-rate allowance provided for in Article 3 (1) (a) of the Royal Decree of 30 October 2020 laying down the detailed rules for granting exceptional federal financial aid to hospitals in the context of the Coronavirus COVID-19 epidemic for the COVID-19 preparation of the hospital shall be fixed globally at EUR 130.12 per FTE for the three amounts mentioned. This flat-rate allowance shall be granted only once. Article 2. The flat-rate allowance provided for in Article 3 (1) (b) of the same decision, for the non-intensive care to COVID-19 patients, shall be fixed at EUR 144.10 per day of nursing for the patient on a non-intensive bed. Article 3. The flat-rate allowance provided for in Article 3, § 1 (c) of the same decision, for the care to non-COVID-19 patients, shall be fixed at EUR 165,96 per FTE and per full calendar year at EUR 144.10 per day of the coronavirus COVID-19 epidemic.
