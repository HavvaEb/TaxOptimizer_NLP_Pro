The Royal Decree extending the mission of the COVID-19 Commissioner and COVID-19 adjunct Commissioner FILIP, King of the Belgians, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greetings. Having regard to the Constitution, Article 37; Having regard to the Royal Decree of 9 October 2020 appointing the COVID-19 Commissioner and COVID-19 adjunct Commissioner and determining their assignment, Article 1, paragraph 4; On the nomination of the Prime Minister, the Minister of Health and the Minister of the Interior, We have decided and decide: Article 1. The mission of the COVID-19 Commissioner is extended for a period of six months. The commission of the CoVid-19 adjunct Commissioner is extended for a period of six months.
