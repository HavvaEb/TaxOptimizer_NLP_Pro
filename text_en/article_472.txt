Decision of the Government of the French Community amending the Decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 concerning various provisions relating to civil service within the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic The Government of the French Community, having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 on the reform of the institutions, Article 87 (3) replaced by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; Having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 22 July 1996 laying down the Staff Regulations of officials of the Services of the Government of the French Community; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 2 June 2004 concerning the privileges and absences of the servants of the Services of the Government of the French Community, the High Council for the Audiovisual Sector and the institutions of the Sector XVII1. In Article 7, paragraph 1, of the Decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 on various provisions relating to civil service in the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic, replaced by the decisions of the Government of the French Community of 2 July 2020, 3 September 2020, 29 October 2020, 10 December 2020, 7 January 2021 and 11 February 2021, the words " 30 April 2021 " shall be replaced by the words " 30 June 2021 ". Article 2. This Decision shall enter into force on 1 May 2021. Article 3. The Minister of Civil Service shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 22 April 2021. For the Government: The Prime Minister, P.-Y. JEHOLET The Minister of the Budget, Civil Service, Equal Opportunities and the Supervision of " Wallonie Bruxelles Enseignement," Fr.
