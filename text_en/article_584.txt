Law conferring a right to a small fee for workers in order to receive a vaccine to protect against the coronavirus COVID-19 (1) FILIP, King of Belgium, To all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. The Chamber of Representatives has adopted and We confirm what follows: CHAPTER I. - General provision and scope of application Article 1. This law governs a matter as referred to in Article 74 of the Constitution. Art. 2. This law applies to all employees and employers connected by an employment contract. CHAPTER II. - Right to paid absence from work Art. 3. The worker has the right to be absent from work, while maintaining his normal wage, with the purpose of obtaining a vaccine to protect against the coronavirus COOVID-19. The worker shall have this right during the period necessary for the vaccination. The absence of the first paragraph of the work shall be treated with an absence of work within the meaning of Article 30 (1) of the Law of 3 July 1978 concerning the employment agreements.The King may, following the opinion of the National Labour Council, by a decision adopted after consultation in the Council of Ministers, postpone the date of non-entry referred to in the first paragraph until 30 June 2022. Announce this law, order that it be held with the Land's seal and will be published by the Belgian Official Journal. Given in Brussels, 28 March 2021. FILIP Van Koningswege : The Minister of Work, P.-Y. DERMAGNE With's Land's seal sealed : The Minister of Justice, V.
