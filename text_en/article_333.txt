This decision of the Flemish Government to grant an optional grant to the airport operating company Antwerpen in the framework of COVID-19 for the financial year 2020 Legal basis This decision is based on: - the Law of 16 May 2003 laying down the general provisions applicable to the budgets, the control of grants and the accounts of the communities and the regions, as well as on the organisation of the audit by the Court of Auditors; - the Decree of 26 June 2020 adjusting the general budget of the Flemish Community for the financial year 2020; - the Decision of the Flemish Government of 2 October 2019 determining the powers of the members of the Flemish Government; - the Flemish Codex Public Finances of 29 March 2019; - the Decision of the Flemish Codex Public Finance of 17 May 2019. Formal requirements The following formal requirement will be fulfilled: - The Treasury has given its opinion on 26 June 2020. - The Flemish Minister responsible for budgetary policy, has given his agreement on 9 July 2020.The Flemish Minister responsible for Mobility and Public Works, L. PEETERS, is responsible for implementing this decision. Brussels, 10 July 2020. The Prime Minister of the Flemish Government, J. JAMBON The Flemish Minister for Mobility and Public Works, L. PEETERS
