Decision of the Walloon Government of 24 March concerning the granting of an additional concession to the establishments which have been taken by decision in the context of the crisis in connection with the Coronavirus COVID-19 The Walloon Government, having regard to the Decree of 11 March 2004 on regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises, and in particular Articles 10 and 19 thereof; having regard to the report of 2 April 2021 drawn up in accordance with Article 3 2° of the Decree of 11 April 2014 implementing the resolutions of the United Nations Women's Conference in Beijing in September 1995 and integrating the gender dimension into the whole of regional policies; having regard to the opinion of the Treasury Inspector, given on 2 April 2021; having regard to the agreement of the Minister of the Budget, given on 8 April 2021; having regard to the opinion of the Council of State, given on 15 April 2021, pursuant to Article 84, § 1, 3° of the Laws of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; having regard to the opinion of the Minister for Budget, given on 8 April 2021;On the basis of the proposal of the Minister of Economy, Decision: Article 1. For the purposes of this Decision, the following shall mean: 1° the Decree: the Decree of 11 March 2004 concerning regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises; 2° the Minister: the Minister responsible for Economy; 3° the undertaking: the very small, small or medium-sized undertaking referred to in Article 3 (3) and (5) of the Decree, as well as the natural person exercising an activity in main or secondary occupation and who, taking into account his professional income, is required to pay social contributions; 4° the NACE-BEL Code: the nomenclature of activities developed by the National Statistical Institute (NACE-BEL 2008) in a single European context, imposed by Regulation (EEC) No 3037/90 of 9 October 1990 of the Council on the statistical nomenclature of economic activities in the European Community, as amended by Commission Regulation (EEC) No 761/93 of 24 March 1993, by Regulation (EC) No 292/2002 of 19 December 2001 by Regulation (EC) No 1882/2003 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 29 September 2003 (EC) of the European Parliament and of the Council of 20 December of the Council of the European Communities.of 18 December 2013 on the application of Articles 107 and 108 of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union to de minimis aid, hereinafter referred to as "Regulation No 1407/2013'. Article 4. The Minister shall, in accordance with the detailed rules which he lays down, grant an additional concession to the undertaking which owned a branch unit referred to in Article I.2, 16°, of Book I of the Economic Law Code and whose activity is covered by one of the NACE BEL codes listed in one of the sectors or sub-sectors referred to in the following sub-classes: 1° 47.990 of the NACE BEL code; 2° 59.140 of the NACE BEL code; 3° 82.300 of the NACE BEL code; 4° 85.510 of the NACE BEL code; 5° 85.520 of the NACE BEL code; 6° 90.021 of the NACE BEL code; 7° 90.041 of the NACE BEL code; 8° 90.042 of the NACE BEL code; 9° 91.030 of the NACE BEL code;The Minister may add sectors or sub-sectors referred to in paragraph 1 to the extent that they were closed at least until 27 March 2021 on the basis of a federal or regional measure taken to combat the COVID-19 epidemic.Article 6 The additional concession referred to in Articles 4 and 5 may not be granted to a company registered in the Cross Point Bank of Enterprises only once in accordance with Book III, Title 2, Chapter 1 of the Code of Economic Law. The undertaking requesting the additional concession referred to in Articles 4 and 5 shall undertake not to pay a dividend in the year 2021. Article 7 Within the time limits laid down by the Minister, the undertaking shall submit its application for additional concession to the Administration by means of a form on the web platform. When submitting the file on the web platform, the undertaking shall provide at least the following information: 1° its number of the Cross Point Bank of Enterprises; 2° the NACE-BEL code for the activity for which the undertaking applies for the additional concession; 3° a statement on the basis of the information to be completed on the web platform; 4° the undertaking's account number, in particularly through the statement referred to in paragraph 3, and the statement on the basis of the application referred to in Regulation 1407/2013, namely that it shall not apply to the application for an additional concession under Article 5 of the application;Article 10. This Decision shall enter into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal. Art. 11. The Minister of Economy is responsible for the implementation of this Decision.
