Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 28 implementing the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis, conferring a mandate to the Ecureuil Fund The Government of the French Community, having regard to the Decree of 20 June 2002 establishing the 'Fonds Ecureuil de la Communauté française', Article 4, as amended by the Decree of 15 December 2006 and 14 November 2008; having regard to the Decree of 18 December 2019 laying down the revenue budget of the French Community for the financial year 2020, having regard to the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 April 2020,This Decision shall enter into force on the day of its signature. Article 3. The Minister for the Budget shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 18 June 2020. For the Government: The Prime Minister, P.-Y. JEHOLET The Minister for Budget, Civil Service, Equal Opportunities and the Monitoring of WBE, FR.
