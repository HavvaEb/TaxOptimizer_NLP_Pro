Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 16 concerning the support of youth homes, meeting and reception centres, youth information centres and youth organisations in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis The Government of the French Community, Having regard to the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis, Article 1 (1) (d) ; Having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance of 21 April 2020; Having regard to the agreement finding by the Minister of Budget of 30 April 2020; Having regard to the gender test of 24 April 2020 carried out pursuant to Article 4 (2) (1) of the Decree of 7 January 2016 on the integration of the gender dimension into all policies of the French Community; Having regard to opinion No 67.350/2 of the Council of State delivered on 8 May 2020, pursuant to Article 84 (1) of the Laws of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973;Whereas the health crisis resulting from this situation, in particular in Belgium; whereas the health measures taken in the context of the containment have the effect of delaying the administrative work of, on the one hand, youth centres, meeting and reception centres and youth information centres and, on the other hand, youth organisations in the context of the evaluation of the four-year action plan for the former and in the framework of the procedure for renewing the recognition, classification and admission to a special system for the second; whereas the strict time limits laid down in Articles 9, 12, 14 and 17 of the Government Decision of 5 December 2008 laying down the detailed rules for the application of the Decree of 20 May 2000 laying down the conditions for the recognition and subsidising of youth housing establishments, meeting and housing centres, of information centres for young people and of their federations are laid down in Articles 7, 9, 12 and 14 of the Government Decision of 27 May 2009 laying down the detailed rules for the application of the decree of 26 March 2009 determining the conditions for the recognition and subsidy of the youth organizations, and the time limits for the submission of information centres for the latter's documents and their associations;Article 12 of the above Decision of 5 December 2008 shall be extended until 15 October 2020 for applications for evaluation of the action plan submitted in 2020. Article 13 of the above Decision of 5 December 2008 shall be extended until 25 October 2020 for applications for evaluation of the action plan submitted in 2020. Article 12 of the above Decision of 5 December 2008 shall be extended until 15 October 2020 for applications for evaluation of the action plan submitted in 2020 until 15 October 2020 for applications for review of the action plan submitted in 2020. Article 12 of the above Decision of 5 December 2008 shall be extended until 15 October 2020 for applications for review of the action plan submitted in 2020.Article 10. The period laid down in Article 13 of the abovementioned Decision of 27 May 2009 shall be extended to 10 working days before 15 October 2020 for applications for renewal of the approval, classification and admission to a special system submitted in 2020. Article 11. The period laid down in Article 14 of the abovementioned Decision of 27 May 2009 shall be extended to 20 December 2020 for applications for renewal of the approval, classification and admission to a special system submitted in 2020. Article 12. The period laid down in Article 8 of the abovementioned Decision of 27 May 2009 shall be extended to 31 January 2021 for applications for renewal of the approval, classification and admission to a special system submitted in 2020. CHAPTER 3. - Final provisions Article 13. This Decision shall enter into force on the day on which it is signed. Article 14. The Minister of Youth shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 28 May 2020.
