Royal Decree declaring the collective agreement of 23 November 2020 concluded in the Joint Subcommittee on Family and Older People's Assistance of the French Community, the Walloon Region and the German-speaking Community on temporary unemployment for the calculation of the final annual grant of 2020 in the context of the sanitary crisis Covid-19 in the services of family and elderly assistance of the French Community, the Walloon Region and the German-speaking Community (1) FILIP, King of Belgians, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. In accordance with the law of 5 December 1968 concerning collective agreements and joint committees, in particular Article 28; In view of the request of the Joint Subcommittee on Family and Older People's Assistance Services of the French Community, the Walloon Region and the German-speaking Community concerning the nomination of the Minister of Work, Hebben We decide and decide: Article 1.In the case of family and elderly workers, the French Community, the Walloon Region and the German-speaking Community, which are recognized and subsidised by the Walloon Region. 'Workers' means the male and female servants who have the task of providing assistance to families and elderly persons. Art. 2. Context This collective agreement is concluded in the context of the Covid-19 crisis. Art. 3. Subject For the calculation of the amount of the flat-rate and variable part of the 2020 end-of-year premium as provided for in the collective agreement of 5 February 2020 granting a final year's premium to the servants, this agreement shall be assimilated to effective working days, including, for the period from 1 March 2020 to 30 September 2020, the days of interruption of employment due to temporary unemployment due to force majeure and temporary unemployment due to force majeure due to the corona virus (corona unemployment).
