In the light of the opinion of the Board of State for Employment on 12 November 2020, given on 26 November 2020, and in the light of Article 8 (2) and (2) of the Law on full unemployment benefits, and in the light of the opinion of the Finance Minister, given on 12 October 2020;Article 1 of the Royal Decree of 23 April 2020 and 27 September 2020, as amended by the Royal Decrees of 25 July 2020 and 27 September 2020, the date "30 September 2020' is replaced by the date "31 December 2020'. In Article 5 of the same Decree of 23 April 2020, as amended by the Royal Decrees of 25 July 2020 and 27 September 2020, the first paragraph is replaced by the words "with six months'. In Article 5 of the same Decree of 23 April 2020, as amended by the Royal Decrees of 25 July 2020 and 27 September 2020, the words "with six months' are replaced by the words "with nine months' and in the second and third paragraph the date "30 September 2020' is replaced by the date '31 December 2020'.
