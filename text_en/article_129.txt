The Agreement between the competent authorities of the Netherlands and Belgium extending the Agreement on the situation of frontier workers in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis of 30 April 2020, as extended by the Agreements of 19 May 2020, 19 June 2020 and 24 August 2020 1. Introduction On 30 April 2020, the Netherlands and Belgium concluded an Agreement on the basis of Article 28, third paragraph, of the Convention signed on 5 June 2001 between the Kingdom of the Netherlands and the Kingdom of Belgium on the avoidance of double taxation and the avoidance of tax on income and wealth, as amended by the Protocol signed on 23 June 2009 ("the Agreement'). This Agreement was published in the Dutch Official Journal of 8 May 2020, No 25956, and in the Belgian Official Journal of 14 May 2020, edition 1, p. 34071.van Nederland: R.
