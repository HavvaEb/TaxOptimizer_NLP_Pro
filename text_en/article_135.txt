The Royal Decree derogating from Article 5 (2) of the Royal Decree of 24 March 2003 on the granting of an allowance to the representative trade unions referred to in Article 7 of the Law of 19 December 1974 on the settlement of relations between the government and the trade unions of its staff, in the context of the health crisis caused by the Coronavirus COVID-19 FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, our Greet. Having regard to Article 37 of the Constitution; having regard to the Royal Decree of 17 July 1991 on the coordination of laws on the National Comptability; having regard to the Royal Decree of 24 March 2003 on the granting of an allowance to the representative trade unions referred to in Article 7 of the Law of 19 December 1974 on the settlement of relations between the government and the trade unions of its staff; having regard to the exemption from the regulatory analysis provided for in Article 8, § 1, 4°, of the Law of 15 December 2013 laying down various provisions on administrative simplification; having regard to the opinion of the Treasury, given on 29 May 2020;the year 2019 grant established on 31 July 2020. Article 2. This decision has effect from 31 March 2020. Article 3. The Minister responsible for Civil Service Affairs is responsible for the implementation of this decision. Given in Brussels, 15 July 2020. FILIP Van Koningswege: The Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Civil Service, D. CLARINVAL
