The Regional Administration of Brussels Fiscality is subject to the provisions of the Brussels Capital Government's decision of 21 March 2018 concerning the administrative status and remuneration of officials of the regional government of Brussels. The Brussels Capital Government's application of book IV of the above-mentioned decision declares the relationship described below vacant and transfers it to the public call for candidacy for this relationship. The mandate holder shall be designated by the Brussels Capital Government. The term of office shall be set at five years. The holder of the mandate shall be evaluated during the exercise and after the end of his/her term of office. Brussels Fiscality shall have from the beginning a very clear mission: on the one hand the fiscal powers properly exercised, on the other hand the customer-oriented and effective management of the following decision shall be designated by the Brussels Capital Government.The following shall not be used: - an explanation of the titles and merits presented by the candidate/candidate to apply for a post, using the standardized CV as provided for in Article 445 (3) of the above Decision and the model of which was laid down by the Minister The candidates whose application has been declared admissible by the selection committee shall be invited for a interview, including an assessment test, as provided for in Article 452 of the said Decision.The assessment test shall consist of a set of simulation exercises intended to verify whether someone has the required skills and capacities for a specific relationship.After the conclusion of the interview, the selection committee shall take account of a reasoned opinion for each candidate/candidate with: - the profile of the candidate/candidate with the job description, tested after the interview; - the qualifications and achievements which the candidate/candidate allows for a specific relationship.Brussels
