Invitation to candidates through mobility to the Brussels Capital Service for Fire and Urgent Medical Assistance (DBDMH) The first assistant (m/v/x) to the remuneration service (grade B2) in the Dutch language framework, in accordance with the language laws, has been declared vacant to fill in through intra-regional or external mobility pursuant to the decision of the Brussels Capital Government of 27 March 2014 regulating mobility in certain institutions of the Brussels Capital Region.
