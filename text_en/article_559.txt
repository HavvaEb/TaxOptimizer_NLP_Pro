Special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/014 concerning the tax on taxi services and services for the hire of vehicles with drivers on the basis of the health crisis of the COVID-19 The Brussels Capital Government, having regard to the order of 19 March 2020 to grant special powers to the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the health crisis COVID-19, Article 2; having regard to the order of 27 April 1995 concerning taxi services and services for the rental of vehicles with drivers, Articles 13 and 26, § 1; having regard to the order of 21 December 2012 establishing the tax procedure in the Brussels Capital Region, Article 14; whereas, in application of Article 2, § 3, 5° of Regulation of 4 October 2018 introducing the equal opportunity test, it is not necessary to draw up the equivalent text for which the opinion of the Council of State is requested by reason of urgency, taking into account the fact that the fact that the health crisis caused by COVID and its operators with regard to the provision of the services of this type of services within theArticle 26 (1) of the same decree is not due for the tax year 2020. Art. 3. By way of derogation from Article 14 of the Ordinance of 21 December 2012 establishing the tax procedure in the Brussels Capital Region, this Decision shall not be liable for the tax year 2020.
