Decision of the Walloon Government on the granting of a specific grant in favour of establishments closed by decision in the context of the crisis in connection with the Coronavirus COVID-19 The Walloon Government, having regard to the Decree of 11 March 2004 on regional incentives in favour of small and medium-sized enterprises, and in particular Articles 10 and 19 thereof; having regard to the report of 26 October 2020 drawn up in accordance with Article 3 2° of the Decree of 11 April 2014 implementing the resolutions of the United Nations Women's Conference in Beijing in September 1995 and integrating the gender dimension in the whole of regional policies; having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance given on 28 October 2020; having regard to the approval of the Minister of the Budget, given on 29 October 2020; having regard to opinion 68.212/2 of the Council of State, given on 4 November 2020, given in accordance with paragraph 1 of Article 84 of the Laws of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; having regard to the urgency;Whereas these problems and effects will be felt in the very short term by Parliament and therefore it is not justified to delay the implementation of the aid measure; whereas it is essential that such aid be paid without delay; to the proposal of the Minister of Economy; after discussion, Decision: Article 1. For the purposes of this Decision, the following shall mean: 1° Decree: the Decree of 11 March 2004 concerning regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises; 2° Minister: the Minister of Economy; 3° undertaking: the very small, small or medium-sized undertaking referred to in Article 3, §§ 3 and 5 of the Decree, as well as the natural person engaged in an activity in primary or secondary occupation and who, taking into account his economic activities in the European Community, shall pay social contributions; 4° NACE-BEL code: the activity nomenclature drawn up by the National Institute for Statistics (NACE-BEL 2008) in a single European context, imposed by Regulation (EEC) No 3037/90 of 9 October 1990 concerning the statistical nomenclature of economic activities in the European Community, as amended by Regulation (EEC) No.The Minister shall, in accordance with the detailed rules laid down by him, grant a specific concession to the undertaking which owned, before 19 October 2020, an establishment unit as referred to in Article I.2, 16°, of Book I of the Economic Law Code and whose activity is covered by one of the NACE BEL codes in one of the sectors or sub-sectors referred to in the following sub-classes: 1) 56.101 of NACE BEL code;
