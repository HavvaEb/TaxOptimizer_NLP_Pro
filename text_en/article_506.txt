Decision of the Government of the French Community amending the Decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 concerning various provisions relating to civil service within the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic The Government of the French Community, having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 on the reform of the institutions, had replaced Article 87 (3) by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 22 July 1996 concerning the Staff Regulations of officials of the Government of the French Community; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 2 June 2004 concerning the privileges and absences of staff of the Government of the French Community, the High Council for the Audiovisual Sector and the institutions of public utility under the Committee of Sector XVII; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 15 November 2017 on telework; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 concerning various provisions concerning civil service in the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic, amended by the decisions of the Government of the Government of the French Government of 2 July 2020;Article 2 of the Decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 concerning various provisions relating to civil service in the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic, paragraph 2 shall be supplemented by the following paragraphs: "By way of derogation from the first paragraph, the hierarchical superior may organise the planning of the performance of his team without guaranteeing the physical presence of each member of staff at the workplace for at least two days a week, if their presence is not absolutely necessary to maintain the quality of the service, the working dynamics of the team or the social cohesion of the service. In such cases, the hierarchical superior shall nevertheless take the necessary measures to ensure that the presence of each member of staff in his team is as far as possible in the direction of the two days presence at the workplace per week.
