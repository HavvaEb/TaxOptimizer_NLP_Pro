For the purposes of this Decision: 1° Minister: the Prime Minister of the Brussels Capital Government, responsible for the remuneration of passenger transport services; 2° operators: any legal person or natural person who has a licence for the operation of a taxi service or a service for the operation of vehicles with driver referred to in Articles 4 and 17 of the Ordinance of 27 April 1995 concerning the hire of vehicles with driver;The Commission's decision of 8 October 2015 concerning the retention, recovery and non-payment of subsidies in respect of employment and economy, as provided for in Article 3 (1) of Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71, shall be taken into account in the application of Article 3 (1) of Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71.Article 6. Within three months of receipt of the application for aid, the grant decision shall be served on the beneficiary. The Minister may extend the decision-making period if no budget appropriations are available. BM shall inform the beneficiary that the aid is granted in accordance with the de minimis regulation. Article 7. The aid shall be paid in a single instalment on a Belgian account in the name of the beneficiary. Article 8. The Prime Minister responsible for Housing, Territorial Development and Urban Renewal, Tourism, the Promotion of the Imago of Brussels and Bicultural Affairs of Regional Interest and for the Paid Passenger Transport and the Minister of Economy shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Article 9. This decision shall enter into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal. Brussels, 10 June 2021.
