The decree of temporary derogation from the Flemish Codex Fiscality as a result of the corona crisis (1) The VLAAMS PARLIAMENT has adopted and We, GOVERNMENT, confirm the following: DECREET to temporary derogation from the Flemish Codex Fiscality as a result of the corona crisis CHAPTER 1. - General provision Article 1. This decree regulates a regional issue. CHAPTER 2. - Temporary amendment of the Flemish Codex Fiscality of 13 December 2013 Article 2. For the purposes of Article 2.7.3.3.2, first paragraph, 3°, of the Flemish Codex Fiscality of 13 December 2013, directors of a death, in addition to the dates mentioned in Article 2.7.3.3.2, second paragraph, of the same decree, may choose from the stock exchange value at the date of three months after the death, to the extent that such death has been established: 1° from 13 November 2019 to 30 September 2020, if the death occurred in the Rijk; 2° from 13 October 2019 to 30 September 2020, if the decease occurs in a country belonging to the European Economic Space; 3° from 13 September 2019 to 30 September 2020, if the decease has occurred in the European Space State in accordance with the first paragraph of Article 1.The complainant of the complaint and the supervisory authority in the context of the administrative supervision of the local government and mentioned in Article 1, 2°, (a) of the decision of the Flemish Government of 7 September 2018 determining the method of communication between the provincial government, the complainant of the complaint and the supervisory authority in the framework of the administrative control of the provincial government. § 2. The deadline of 15 May, mentioned in Article 3.0.0.4, § 5, 2°, of the same decree, shall be extended to 31 May 2020 only. By way of derogation from Article 3.0.0.4, § 5, 2°, of the same decree, it shall be sufficient for the municipality, respectively, of the agglomeration or the province, of the estimated amount of annual receipts of the advance payment to the competent authority.
