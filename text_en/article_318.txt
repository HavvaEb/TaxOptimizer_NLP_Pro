Decision of the Walloon Government amending the decision of the Walloon Government of 24 September 2020 establishing a category of cyclically protected customers of electricity and gas on a short-term basis within the framework of the COVID-19 crisis The Walloon Government, having regard to the Decree of 12 April 2001 concerning the organisation of the regional electricity market, Article 33 (2) as amended by the Decree of 11 April 2014; having regard to the Decree of 19 December 2002 concerning the organisation of the regional gas market, Article 31a (2), last amended by the Decree of 21 May 2015; having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 24 September 2020 establishing a category of cyclically protected customers of electricity and gas in the short term in the context of the COVID-19 crisis, last amended by the Decision of 1 April 2021; having regard to the report of 29 November 2021, drawn up in accordance with Article 3, 2°, of the Decree of 11 April 2014 implementing the resolutions of the United Nations Conference of the United Nations in Beijing in September 1995 and to the integration of the gender dimension in the entirety of the regional policies,The Walloon Government of 24 September 2020 establishing a category of cyclically protected customers of electricity and gas in the short term in the context of the COVID-19 crisis shall be replaced by the following: " (d) a customer with a damage certificate from the insurance company of the customer following the floods of July 2021 or an acknowledgement of receipt of an aid application from the Rampen Fund as a result of the floods of July 2021." Article 2. In Article 2 of the same Decision, the words "in 2020 and in 2021" shall be replaced by the words "in 2020, 2021 and 2022." Article 3 of the same Decision shall be replaced by the words "in 2020, 2021 and 2022." Article 3 of the same Decision shall be amended as follows: (a) in the first paragraph, the words "31 December 2021" shall be replaced by the words "31 August 2022; (b) in paragraph 3, 2°, the words "31 December 2021" shall be replaced by the words "31 August 2021" shall be replaced by the words "31 August 2022."
