Decision of the Walloon Government of 30 June 2021 of the provisions of the Walloon Government of 30 June 2020 laying down various provisions relating to civil service in the context of the coronavirus pandemic and the gradual reconstruction of activities The Walloon Government, having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 reforming the institutions, in particular Article 87 (3) thereof, replaced by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; Having regard to the Walloon Government's decision of 18 December 2003 concerning the Walloon Civil Service Code, and in particular Articles 361 and following thereof, as amended; Having regard to the Walloon Government's decision of 13 September 2011 on teleworking, as amended by the Walloon Government's decision of 31 January 2013, for its still applicable provisions; Having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 5 December 2019 concerning teleworking; Having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 13 September 2019 determining the distribution of the ministerial powers and regulating the signature of its instruments, as amended;This Regulation shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
