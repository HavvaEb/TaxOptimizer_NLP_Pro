Decision of the Brussels Capital Government amending the decision of the Brussels Capital Government of 11 February 2021 laying down urgent measures in the field of mobility and road safety to reduce the spread of COVID-19; - 2nd reading The Brussels Capital Government, having regard to the law on police on road traffic coordinated on 16 March 1968, Article 1, replaced by the law of 21 June 1985 and amended by the law of 28 April 2010, and Article 23, replaced by the law of 9 July 1976 and amended by the laws of 29 February 1984 and 18 July 1990; Having regard to the law of 18 February 1969 concerning measures to implement the international conventions and instruments on maritime transport, road, rail or waterway, Article 1, replaced by the laws of 21 June 1985, 28 July 1987 and 15 May 2006, as amended by Law of 8 May 2019;in the field of mobility and road safety in order to reduce the spread of COVID-19, the words "30 June" shall be replaced by the words "31 December'. Article 3. This Decision shall enter into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal. Article 4. The Minister responsible for Mobility and Road Safety shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 15 July 2021, For the Brussels Capital Government: The Prime Minister of the Brussels Capital Government, R. PROPOSES the Minister of the Brussels Capital Government, responsible for Mobility, Public Works and Road Safety, E. VAN DEN BRANDT
