Special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/046 amending the special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/010 of 30 April 2020 concerning a delegated mission to the Regional Investment Company for Brussels (GIMB) for the granting of credits to certain undertakings in the hospitality sector or for its supply because of the COOVID-19 health crisis The Brussels Capital Government, having regard to the decree of 19 March 2020 granting special powers to the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the COOVID-19 health crisis, Article 2 (1); having regard to Article 2, § 3, 5°, of the decree of 4 October 2018 introducing the equal opportunities test; having regard to the special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/010 of 30 April 2020 concerning a delegated mandate to the Regional Investment Company for Brussels (GIMB) for the granting of loans to certain undertakings in the hospitality sector or for the purpose of granting of the loan to the loan of COVID;Inspection of Finance and of the Economic and Social Council, as authorised by Article 2 (4) of the Order of 19 March 2020 granting special powers to the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis; On a proposal from the Minister of Economy; After deliberation, Decision, Article 1. In Article 7 (2), first paragraph 2°, of the special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/010 of 30 April 2020 concerning a delegated mandate to the Regional Investment Company for Brussels (GIMB) for the granting of credits to certain undertakings in the hospitality sector or for its supply because of the COVID-19 health crisis, the words "at least 50 persons" shall be replaced by the words "at least 10 persons."
