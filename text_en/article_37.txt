Law suspending the notice period for dismissals given before or during the period of temporary suspension of the performance of the contract of employment for reasons of force majeure pursuant to the COVID-19 crisis FILIP, King of Belgium, To all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. The Chamber of Representatives has adopted and We confirm the following: Article 1. This Act regulates a matter referred to in Article 74 of the Constitution. Art. 2. Both the employee and the employer may terminate the employment contract during the suspension of the contract due to temporary force majeure resulting from the measures taken by the Government to restrict the dissemination of the coronavirus COVID-19. In the event of termination by the employee prior to or during the suspension provided for in the first paragraph, the notice period of suspension shall run before or during that period of suspension shall be suspended by the latter.
