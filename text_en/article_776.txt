Royal Decree amending Act of 23 March 2020 amending the Law of 22 December 2016 introducing a transitional right in favour of self-employed persons and introducing temporary measures in the framework of COVID-19 in favour of self-employed persons FILIP, King of Belgians, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. Having regard to the Law of 23 March 2020 amending the Law of 22 December 2016 introducing a transitional right in favour of self-employed persons and introducing temporary measures in the framework of COVID-19 in favour of self-employed persons, Article 6 (2); Having regard to the opinion of the General Management Committee for the Social Status of Self-employed persons, given on 12 May 2020; having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 11 May 2020; Having regard to the agreement of the Minister of Budget, given on 13 May 2020; Having regard to the Article 8 of the Law of 15 December 2013 laying down various provisions in respect to administrative simplification, this Decision is exempt from regulatory analysis in view of the urgency of theThe Minister of Social Affairs and the Minister of Self-Employees must be able to inform about the measure adopted and to take the necessary decisions on the right of bridging very quickly; On the nomination of the Minister of Social Affairs and the Minister of Self-Employees and on the opinion of the Ministers meeting in Council, We have decided and decide: Article 1. In Article 6, § 1, 2° to 4°, of the Law of 23 March 2020 amending the Law of 22 December 2016 introducing a right of bridging in favour of self-employed persons and introducing temporary measures under COVID-19 in favour of self-employed persons, the words "in the period from 1 March 2020 to 31 May 2020" shall be replaced by the words "in the period from 1 March 2020 to 30 June 2020."
