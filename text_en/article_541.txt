Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 22 concerning aid to the sector of education for social promotion in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis The Government of the French Community, having regard to the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis, and in particular Article 1 (1) (g) thereof, having regard to the opinion of the Finance Inspectorate, given on 8 May 2020; Having regard to the agreement of the Minister of Budget of 20 May 2020; Having regard to the opinion of the Council of State 67.465/2, given on 28/05/2020, applying Article 84, paragraph 1, paragraph 1, 3°, of the Laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; Whereas the health crisis in connection with the COVID-19 has caused major economic problems for social promotional institutions to organize additional periods of training required by Article 84 (1) of the Board of State;Social promotion grants the institutions the teaching times referred to in Article 3 following the advice of the General Council of Education for Social Promotion with regard to the distribution of resources between the various institutions. If the number of applications exceeds the number of teaching hours available, the Minister of Education for Social Promotion gives priority to the teaching units of departments which relate to a critical profession or a problem point occupation. Art. 5. This decision enters into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal. Art. 6. The Minister of Education for Social Promotion is responsible for the implementation of this decision. Brussels, 11 June 2020. The Minister-President, P.-Y.
