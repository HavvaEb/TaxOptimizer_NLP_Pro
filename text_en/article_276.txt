The Ministerial Decision amending the Ministerial Decision of 27 November 2017 determining the fees for the provision of information, certificates, copies and extracts by the General Administration of the Patrimony Documentation in implementation of the Code of Registration, Mortgage and Registry Rights and the Code of Succession Rights The Vice-First Minister and Minister of Finance, having regard to the Code of Succession Rights, and in particular Articles 90, 143 to 146 and 160 thereof; Having regard to the Code of Registration, Hypotheque and Registry Rights, and in particular Article 236 thereof; Having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 21 February 2020; Having regard to the agreement of the Minister of Budget, given on 23 April 2020; Having regard to the opinion of the Council of State, given on 28 May 2020, pursuant to Article 84, paragraph 1, 2° of the Laws of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, amended by the Act of 20 January 2014, Decision: Article 1 of the Ministerial Decision of 27 November 2017 granting to the Decision of the Secretary of the Secretary of State, to the Secretary of State, and to the Secretary of State,10 days from the day following its publication in the Belgian Official Journal. Brussels, 2 June 2020. A. DE CROO
