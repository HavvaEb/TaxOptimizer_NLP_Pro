Law for the charging of the costs of the urgent transport of the COVID-19 patients FILIP, King of the Belgians, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greeting. The Chamber of Representatives of the People has adopted and We confirm the following: Article 1. This law regulates a matter as referred to in Article 74 of the Constitution. Article 2. Article 101 of the law on hospitals and other care facilities coordinated on 10 July 2008, as last amended by the Royal Decree of 19 April 2020, is supplemented by a member, which states: "The patients or victims of a pandemic or disaster may not be charged for urgent transportation." Art. 3. This law has effect with effect from 1 March 2020. Announce this law, which stipulates that it will be held by the Belgian State's seal and will be published in Brussels, 3 July 2020. FILIP Van Koningswege: The Minister of Social Affairs and of Health, M. DE BLOCK With's stamp sealed: The Minister of Justice, K. No.
