Royal Decree amending the Royal Decree of 9 November 2020 establishing the College for the fight against tax and social fraud FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greeting. Having regard to Article 37 of the Constitution; having regard to the Royal Decree of 29 April 2008 establishing the Ministerial Committee for the fight against tax and social fraud; having regard to the Royal Decree of 23 October 2020 designating the members of the Ministerial Committee for the fight against tax and social fraud; having regard to Article 2 (1) of the Royal Decree of 9 November 2020 establishing the College for the fight against tax and social fraud; having regard to Article 8 of the Law of 15 December 2013 laying down various provisions on administrative simplification, this Decision is exempt from a regulatory analysis, since it concerns the provisions of autoregulation; having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 13 January 2021; having regard to the approval of the State Secretary for Budget, given on 18 January 2021;
