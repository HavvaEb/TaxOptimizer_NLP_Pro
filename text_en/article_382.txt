For the purposes of this Decision: 1° Decree: the Decree of 11 March 2004 on regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises in favour of small and medium-sized enterprises;(b) the average number of employees employed in 2019 by a contract of employment in the joint companies of the undertaking corresponding to the labour units (JAE) calculated on the basis of multifunctional declarations to the Cross Point Bank of Social Security for the four quarters of 2019;(a) The Minister may add sectors or sub-sectors referred to in paragraph 1, 3° to the extent that they have been prohibited or restricted by a federal or regional measure to comply with the social distance as they involve close individual contacts or bring together too many persons; excluding from the financial concession referred to in paragraph 1, the undertaking which has received a specific concession granted in accordance with the Walloon Government's decision of 22 April 2021 concerning the granting of a specific fee to self-employed persons and undertakings operating in the BtoB sector and is indirectly affected by decisions to close.
