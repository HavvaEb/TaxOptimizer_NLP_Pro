Decision approving the Cooperation Agreement of 31 May 2021 between the Federal State, the Flemish Community, the German-speaking Community, the Walloon Region and the Joint Community Committee on Special Processing of Personal Data for the purpose of detecting and investigating clusters and collectivities, with a view to maintaining the compulsory quarantine and testing and for the purpose of monitoring compliance by the competent social inspectors with the measures to prevent the spread of the Covid-19 coronavirus to the workplaces The Parliament of the German-speaking Community has adopted and We, the Government, confirm as follows: Only Article - In accordance with the Cooperation Agreement of 31 May 2021 between the Federal State, the Flemish Community, the German-speaking Community, the Walloon Region and the Common Commission on Special Processing of Personal Data for the purpose of detecting and investigating clusters and collectivities, with a view to maintaining the quarantine and testing and ensuring compliance by the competent social inspectors of the measures to disseminate the coronavirus COVID-19, and the Common Commission on Special Processing of Personal Data Rights, was issued by the European Parliament on 28 June 2021.
