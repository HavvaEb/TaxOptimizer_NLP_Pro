Decision of the Brussels Capital Government of special powers No 2020/032 extending the measures taken by the Brussels Capital Government of special powers No 2020/012 of 23 April 2020 concerning the introduction of aid measures for approved servicecheques undertakings and their employees following the urgent measures to limit the spread of the Colonavirus COVID-19 REPORT TO THE BRUSSELS Capital GOVERNMENT The Brussels servicecheques sector remains seriously affected by the crisis in May 2020, which makes an extension of the aid measures absolutely necessary. On 23 April 2020, the Brussels Government approved Decision No 2020/012 of the Brussels Capital Government of special powers concerning the introduction of aid measures for approved servicecheques undertakings and their employees in response to the urgent measures to restrict the distribution of the Coronavirus COVID-19.Having regard to Decision No 2020/012 of 23 April 2020 of the Brussels Capital Government of special powers concerning the introduction of aid measures for approved service cheque undertakings and their employees following the urgent measures to restrict the spread of the Covid-19 coronavirus, Articles 4, 7, 10 and 11; having regard to the agreement reached by the Minister of Budget; having regard to Article 2, paragraph 4 of the Order of 19 March 2020 to grant special powers to the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the health crisis COVID-19 which makes it unnecessary to obtain certain opinions, except those of the Council of State; having regard to the opinion No 67,475/1 of the Council of State, given on 2 June 2020, in application of Article 84, paragraph 1, paragraph 3 of the Act of State on 12 January 1973,In Article 4, 1° of the decision of the Brussels-Capital Government No 2020/012 of 23 April 2020 of the Brussels-Capital Government of special powers concerning special powers relating to such undertakings and their employees in the context of the Brussels-Capital Government No 2020/012 of 23 April 2020 of the Brussels-Capital Government of special powers concerning the introduction of State aid to the approved service cheque undertakings and their employees shall be replaced by the words "30 April" in Article 7 of the same Decision of 23 April 2020 of the Brussels-Capital Government of special powers concerning the introduction of State aid to the approved service cheque undertakings and their employees following urgent measures to restrict the distribution of the coronavirus COVID-19 of 30 April.4. In Article 11 of the same Decision No 2020/012 of 23 April 2020 of the Brussels Capital Government of special powers, the following amendments shall be made: 1° in the second paragraph, the words "30 April" shall be replaced by the words "30 June"; 2° in the third paragraph, the words "30 June" shall be replaced by the words "15 September."
