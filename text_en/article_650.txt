Special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/004 to extend the payment period of the property tax to take account of the health crisis caused by the COOVID-19 The Brussels Capital Government, having regard to the order of 19 March 2020 to grant special powers to the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the health crisis COOVID-19, Article 2; having regard to the Order of 6 March 2019 on the Brussels Codex Fiscal Procedure, Article 32; having regard to the equal opportunities test carried out on 25 March 2020 pursuant to Article 2 of the Order of 4 October 2018 introducing the equal opportunities test; having regard to the opinion of the Council of State, given on 15 April 2020, pursuant to Article 84, paragraph 1, 2°, of the laws of the State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; whereas, in application of Article 2, § 4 of the Order of 19 March 2020 to grant special powers to the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the health crisis COVIDBrussels Codex Fiscal Procedure, the payment period for the tax year 2020 is extended to 124 days. This decision has effect with effect from 1 January 2020. Art. 3. The Minister responsible for Finance and Budget is responsible for the implementation of this decision. Brussels, 23 April 2020.
