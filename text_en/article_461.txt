On the basis of the Brussels Government's decision of 18 July 2000 to regulate its operation and to regulate the signature of the Government's acts, as amended by the Government's decisions of 29 November 2001, 30 June 2011, 7 May 2015, and 15 June 2017, pursuant to Article 5, 14°; having regard to the decision of the Brussels Capital Government on the basis of the Brussels Government's decision of 18 July 2000 to regulate its operation and to settle the signature of the Government's acts, as amended by the Government's decisions of 29 November 2001, 30 June 2011, 7 May 2017, and 15 June 2017,This Decision shall enter into force on the twentieth day following that of its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
