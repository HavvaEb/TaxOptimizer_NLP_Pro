Law amending the Law of 4 May 2020 regulating the suspension of the social elections of the year 2020 pursuant to the Coronavirus COVID-19 pandemic to extend the deadline for the conclusion of an agreement to vote electronically in the social elections of 2020 to FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and are listed below, Our Greet. The Chamber of Representatives has adopted and We confirm, as follows: Article 1. This Act regulates a matter referred to in Article 74 of the Constitution. Article 2. In Chapter 3 of the Law of 4 May 2020 regulating the suspension of the procedure of the social elections of the year 2020 pursuant to the coronavirus COVID-19 pandemic, an Article 15/1 shall be inserted, under the following conditions: "Art. 15/1. § 1."Article 8 of the Law of 4 May 2020 regulating the suspension of the procedure of the social elections of the year 2020 pursuant to the coronavirus COVID-19 pandemic shall be replaced by the words "in Article 10" by the words "in Articles 10 and 15/1." Art. 4. This Act shall enter into force on 23 September 2020. Announcement of this Act, which shall be issued with's Lands seal' and shall be published by the Belgian Official Journal in Brussels, 15 July 2020. FILIP Van Koningswege : The Minister of Work, N.
