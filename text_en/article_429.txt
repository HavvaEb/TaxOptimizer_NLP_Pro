Decision approving the decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 16 of 28 May 2020 and We, Government, confirm this: Only Article. The decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 16 of 28 May 2020 concerning the support of youth homes, meeting and reception centres, youth information centres and youth organizations in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis is hereby confirmed in accordance with Article 4 of the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis of the COVID-19 Coronavirus.
