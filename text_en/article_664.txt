Special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/019 amending the Special Power Decision No 2020/013 of 7 April 2020 on aid for the reimbursement of undertakings affected by urgent measures to limit the spread of the Coronavirus COVID-19 Report to the Brussels Capital Government In order to limit the economic damage to companies which are subject to the opening restrictions of the (federal) ministerial decisions of 18 and then 23 March 2020 on urgent measures to limit the spread of the Coronavirus COVID-19, the Government has adopted an urgent decision granting them a one-off premium of EUR 4,000 per unit of establishment, following a thorough assessment of the categories of undertakings which are affected by the federal measure, extending the current decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/013 of 7 April 2020 on aid for the reimbursement of undertakings under the urgent measures to limit the dissemination of the Coronavirus COVID-19.Article 1) an update of a provision referring to the (federal) ministerial decision of 23 March 2020 laying down urgent measures to limit the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19. Indeed, following an amendment of the ministerial decision, the reference was no longer correct.
