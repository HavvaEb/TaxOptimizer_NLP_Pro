Law amending certain provisions of the Code of Economic Law, concerning the extension of the winter soldiers 2021 pursuant to the COVID-19 pandemic (1) FILIP, King of the Belgians, to all who are now and hereafter will be, Our Greeting. The Chamber of Representatives has adopted and We confirm, which is as follows: Article 1. This law regulates a matter referred to in Article 74 of the Constitution. Article 2. In Article VI.25 of the Code of Economic Law, inserted by the law of 21 December 2013 and amended by the law of 27 May 2020, paragraph 4 is replaced as follows: " § 4. For the year 2021, the period referred to in paragraph 1, 1°, shall be extended until 15 February 2021." Art. 3. This law enters into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal.
