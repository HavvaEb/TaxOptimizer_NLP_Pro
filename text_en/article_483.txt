On the basis of Article 275 (1) and (2) of the Act of 24 June 2020 granting a temporary supplement to benefits for primary incapacity for work; in the light of the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, Article 3 (1); in the light of the urgent need; whereas, in accordance with Article 2.12.B (1) of the Act of 22 June 2020 providing for a temporary supplement to benefits for basic incapacity for work which began on 1 March 2020, supplementary allowances for incapacity for work are temporarily granted to workers;This Decision shall apply from 24 June 2020 and shall apply to supplementary benefits paid or granted from that same date. Article 3. The Minister responsible for Finance shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Published in Brussels, 9 July 2020.
