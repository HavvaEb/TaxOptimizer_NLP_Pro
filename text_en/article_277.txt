The decision of the Brussels Capital Government to grant special powers to the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the health crisis COVID-19 The Brussels Capital Parliament has adopted and We, Government, confirm, on 19 March 2020, the following: CHAPTER I. - General provision Article 1. This decree governs a matter as referred to in Article 39 of the Constitution. CHAPTER II. - Confirming measures Art. 2. § 1. The special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/001 of 2 April 2020 concerning the temporary suspension of the lapse and time limits laid down in the Brussels legislation and regulations or which have been adopted pursuant thereto shall be ratified. § 2. The decision of the Brussels Capital Government of 16 April 2020 extending the time limits laid down in Article 1 of the Brussels Capital Decision No 2020/001 of the Brussels Capital Government on the temporary suspension of the periods of lapse and appeals laid down in Brussels legislation and regulations, or on the basis of which shall be adopted pursuant to Article 3.The decision of the Brussels Capital Government on measures relating to mobility, public works and road safety of 16 April 2020 to limit the duration of the exceptional measures provided for in Article 1 of Decision No 2020/002 of the Brussels Capital Government on measures relating to mobility, public works and road safety in order to limit the spread of the coronavirus is hereby confirmed. Article 4. The special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/004 of 23 April 2020 on the operation of the municipal bodies in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis is hereby confirmed.The special power decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/014 of 23 April 2020 concerning the compensation of undertakings made by the urgent measures to limit the spread of the Coronavirus COVID-19 is hereby confirmed.
