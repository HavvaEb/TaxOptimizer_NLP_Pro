Having regard to the opinion of the Finance Inspectorate, issued on 14 July 2021; having regard to the decision of the Minister of Finance, adopted on 16 July 2021; having regard to the opinion of the Council of the European Union on 16 July 2021; having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 1 July 2021 concerning regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises, and in particular Articles 10 and 19 thereof; having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 1 July 2021 concerning the granting of a concession in the form of a safeguard mechanism in the context of the crisis in relation to the Coronavirus COVID-19, and in particular Article 4, paragraph 4 and paragraph 6 thereof; having regard to the report of 12 July 2021 made pursuant to Article 3, 2°, of the Decree of 11 April 2014 implementing the resolutions of the United Nations Women's Conference in Beijing in September 1995 and integrating the gender dimension into the whole of regional policies; having regard to the opinion of the Treasury, issued on 14 July 2021; having regard to the opinion of the Minister of Budget, granted on 16 July 2021;Whereas, moreover, the prospects for a partial or full resumption of the market still depend on the development of the pandemic and the decisions taken by the Consultative Committee; whereas it is urgent to adopt this draft in view of the crisis situation which still exists for those undertakings which are still suffering serious economic damage; whereas the purpose of this aid is in particular to reduce the difficulties encountered and to prevent a wave of bankruptcies from occurring among the undertakings experiencing acute liquidity problems as a result of the crisis; whereas it is therefore necessary to intervene and to grant financial aid to these sectors as soon as possible, Decision: Article 1. For the purposes of this Ministerial Decision, the following shall be understood as: 1° the Walloon Government's decision: the Walloon Government's decision of 1 July 2021 concerning the granting of a concession in the form of a safeguard mechanism in the context of the crisis in connection with the coronavirus COVID-19; 2° the concession: the concession granted in accordance with the decision of the Walloon Government.
