Decision of the Government of the French Community to grant special powers No 3 adopted by the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis of the COVID-19 with regard to the strengthening of the emergency and support fund The Government of the French Community, Having regard to the Decree of 20 December 2011 regulating the budget and the accounts of the Services of the Government of the French Community, Articles 4, § 1, paragraph 2 and 27; having regard to the Decree of 18 December 2019 on the budget for the financial year 2020; having regard to the Decree of 18 December 2019 on the initial revenue budget of the French Community for the financial year 2020; having regard to the Decree of 17 March 2020 conferring special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis COVID-19, Article 1, § 1, g); having regard to the Decision of 7 April 2020 on special powers No 2 of the Government of the French Community to establish an emergency and support fund for the implementation of the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting of special powers to the Government in the context of the COVID-19, paragraph 1 of April 2020;Whereas these and future containment measures taken to limit the spread of the virus in the population are such as to prevent a whole range of activities, that they prevent compliance with the obligations which are a condition for granting grants and that they therefore jeopardise the viability of the beneficiaries of grants; whereas the viability of those engaged on behalf of the French Community should be ensured, irrespective of whether it is a cultural, sporting, associative or other contract, by means of additional expenditure affecting their viability; whereas the expenditure budget for the year 2020 does not permit the use of sufficient resources to enable the French Community to grant financial support to operators whose viability would be impaired as a result of the containment measures, to operators who have incurred additional expenditure in order to endanger their viability and to provide all forms of assistance to families, children and to the public of the Federation in April 2020;Special powers No 2 of the Government of the French Community establishing an emergency and support fund to implement the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis in connection with the COVID-19, the words "EUR 50 000.001" shall be replaced by the words "EUR 80 000.001." Article 2. This Decision shall enter into force on the day of its signature. Article 3. The Minister of Budget shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 23 April 2020.
