Ministerial decision extending the measures linked to the crisis COVID-19 and concerning the temporary prohibition of restrictions on access to public water distribution by one month The Minister of Environment, Nature, Forests, Rural Affairs and Animal Welfare, having regard to the decree-making part of Book II of the Environmental Code, comprising the Water Code, Articles D.202 and D.232; In view of the regulatory part of Book II of the Environmental Code, which includes the Water Code, Article R.270bis-13; In view of the decision of the Walloon Government of 22 April 2020 concerning the temporary prohibition of restrictions on access to public water distribution due to urgent measures to limit the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19; In view of the duration of the health crisis in relation to Covid-19 and the maintenance of various measures to limit the spread of the virus among the population, taking into account of certain restrictions on activities and travel in the framework of the COVID-19, taking into account of the decision of 22 April 2010 concerning the provisional measures taken by the Government on the application of the State to the principle of Article 19, this Decision.
