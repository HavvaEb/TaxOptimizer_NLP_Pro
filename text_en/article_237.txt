Royal Decree No 47 implementing Article 5, § 1, 3° and 5°, of the Law of 27 March 2020 authorising the King to take measures to combat the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19 (II), with a view to granting a temporary premium to those entitled to certain social assistance benefits REPORT TO THE KONING Sire, I have the honour to submit to Your Majesty a draft royal decision to sign to those entitled to certain social assistance benefits a monthly premium of EUR 50 for the months of July 2020 to December 2020. This temporary premium is intended to cover the negative consequences and additional costs of the COVID-19 pandemic for these vulnerable categories.On the basis of Article 14 (1) of the Law of 26 May 2002 on the right to social integration (hereinafter referred to as 'the living wage'), the financial assistance granted under Article 60 (3) of the Organic Law of 8 July 1976 on the public social welfare centres whose aid has been repaid by the State pursuant to Article 1 of the Ministerial Decree of 30 January 1995 on the reimbursement by the State of the costs of the social welfare services granted by the public welfare centres to a person who does not have Belgian nationality and who is not registered in the population register (hereinafter referred to as 'the welfare equivalent to the living wage').In accordance with the Regulation, the right to the premium of the first day of the month following the month in which the person fulfils the legal conditions for such a concession and at the earliest on the first day of the month following the date of submission of the application; the premium shall be 50 euro per month for each beneficiary. Article 4 provides that the premium shall not be granted to persons with disabilities who may obtain it under their right to a GI or an IGO. Rules specific to income beneficiaries and beneficiaries of an aid equivalent to the living wage: This premium shall be granted per month in so far as the beneficiary receives a living wage or an aid equivalent for that month. This premium shall be 50 euro per month for each beneficiary. Entry into force: This decision shall enter into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal. I have the honour to be, Sire, Van Uwe Majesty, the very respectful and faithful servant, The Minister of Justice, K.Having regard to Council of State's opinion No 67.647/1 on 25 June 2020, pursuant to Article 4 (3) of the Law of 27 March 2020 authorizing the King to take measures to combat the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19 (I); whereas the crisis of COVID-19 causes a major economic crisis and additional costs which may lead to poverty; whereas under Article 23 of the Constitution each has the right to lead a dignified life; whereas the need for assistance for the most vulnerable persons; whereas the most vulnerable public is dependent on social assistance benefits; whereas on the proposal of the Minister of Justice, the Minister of Finance, the Minister of Social Affairs, the Minister of Pensions, the Minister of Social Integration and the Minister of Work, charged with the fight against poverty, Equal Opportunities and Persons with a disability, and on the opinion of the Council of Ministers, We have not decided in respect of the condition of the principle of a temporary basis of the principle of the principle of the principle of 1 January 2001;The premium referred to in Article 1, 1° and 2° shall be payable from the date on which the entitlement to a benefit for persons with disabilities is granted under the above Act of 27 February 1987. The premium referred to in Article 1, 3° shall be EUR 50 per month for each beneficiary. Article 4.
