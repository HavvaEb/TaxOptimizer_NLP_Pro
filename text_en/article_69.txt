Special Powers Decision of the Brussels Capital Government No 2020/2005 laying down various measures relating to the taxation of tourist accommodation establishments on the basis of the health crisis of the COVID-19 The Brussels Capital Government, having regard to the order of 19 March 2020 to grant special powers to the Brussels Capital Government in the context of the health crisis Covid-19, Article 2; having regard to the Order of 23 December 2016 concerning the regional tax on tourist accommodation establishments, Articles 3, 6, 7, 8, 9 and 10; having regard to the request for emergency treatment, justified by the fact that the health crisis caused by COVID-19 affects the operators of tourist accommodations, and that it is necessary to take immediate measures to prevent an economic catastrophe.On the proposal of the Minister responsible for Finance, After deliberation, Decision: Article 1. The tax referred to in Article 3 of the Ordinance of 23 December 2016 on the regional tax on tourist accommodation establishments shall not be payable for the occupation of units of accommodation by tourists during the period from 1 January 2020 to 30 June 2020. Article 2. The obligation to proceed with the notification provided for in Article 6 of the same decree shall be suspended for the period from 1 January 2020 to 30 June 2020. Article 3. The officials referred to in Article 7 of the same decree shall not apply to the declaration referred to in Article 8 of the said decree, which shall be submitted for the period from 1 January 2020 to 30 June 2020.Government, responsible for climate transition, environment, energy and participatory democracy, A.
