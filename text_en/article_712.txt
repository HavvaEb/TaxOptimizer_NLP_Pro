Decision of the Brussels Capital Government amending the Royal Decree of 8 July 1970 laying down the general regulation on taxes treated as income tax in the context of the taking up of the service of traffic tax on motor vehicles and the tax on entry by the Brussels Capital Region of the Brussels Capital Government, Having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 on the reform of the institutions, Article 20; Having regard to the Code of taxes treated as income tax, Article 5, paragraph 1, paragraph 2, as amended by the decree of 28 November 2019; Having regard to the Royal Decree of 8 July 1970 on the general regulation on taxes treated as income tax, adopted on 12 February 2020; having regard to the equal opportunities test carried out on 22 November 2019 in application of Article 2 of the Ordinance of 4 October 2018 on the introduction of the equal opportunities test; Having regard to the opinion of the Council of State, given on 12 February 2020, paragraph 1, 2°, of the laws of the State, coordinated on 12 January 1973;the taxes treated as income tax in respect of these taxes must be abolished, in so far as their purpose is directly taken up in the said Codex and its implementing decisions; on the proposal of the Minister of Finance, after consultation, Decision: CHAPTER 1. - Introductory provision Article 1. This Decision shall apply only to: 1° the traffic tax on motor vehicles; 2° the tax on entry. CHAPTER 2. - Changes to the traffic tax on motor vehicles and the tax on entry to which the taxable event falls before 1 January 2020 Article 2. The provisions of this Chapter shall apply exclusively to the tax on motor vehicles and the tax on entry into service of which the taxable event falls before 1 January 2020.(b) the provision under 2° shall be replaced by the following: '2o on motor vehicles used exclusively for the hire of vehicles with drivers under the conditions laid down in the applicable regional regulations and established under a licence for the implementation of the said regulations;' Article 7. The following provisions shall be abolished in the same Decision: 1o the Title I, consisting of Articles 1 to 14; 2° the Chapter III of Title II, consisting of Articles 23 and 24; 3o the Chapter IV of Title II, consisting of Articles 25 and 26; 4o the Chapter V of Title II, consisting of Articles 27 to 29; 5° in the Title V, Article 58, amended by the Royal Decree of 14 April 1993.
