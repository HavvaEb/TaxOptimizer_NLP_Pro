Decree approving the decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 59 of 11 February 2021 concerning the support to the meeting and residence centres in the context of the second wave of the health crisis COVID-19 The Parliament of the French Community has adopted and We, Government, confirm this: Only Article. The decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 59 of 11 February 2021 concerning the support to the meeting and residence centres in the context of the second wave of the health crisis COVID-19, is hereby approved in accordance with Article 3 of the Decree of 14 November 2020 granting special powers to the Government to respond to the second wave of the "COVID-19" health crisis.
