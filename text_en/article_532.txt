Decision of the Walloon Government on the granting of an additional concession to establishments in the catering sector which are closed by decision in the context of the crisis of the coronavirus COVID-19 The Walloon Government, having regard to the Decree of 11 March 2004 on regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises, and in particular Articles 10 and 19 thereof; having regard to the report of 23 March 2021, drawn up in accordance with Article 3 2° of the Decree of 11 April 2014 implementing the resolutions of the United Nations Women's Conference which took place in Beijing in September 1995 and integrating the gender dimension into the whole of the regional policies; having regard to the opinion of the Finance Inspectorate, given on 24 March 2021; having regard to the approval of the Minister of the Budget, given on 25 March 2021; having regard to opinion No 69120/2 of the State, given on 1 April 2021, pursuant to Article 84, paragraph 1, 3°, of the Laws of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; having regard to the decision of the Minister of the Budget, given on 25 March 2021;Commission Regulation (EEC) No 3037/90 of 9 October 1990 concerning the statistical nomenclature of economic activities in the European Community, as amended by Commission Regulation (EEC) No 761/93 of 24 March 1993, by Regulation (EC) No 29/2002 of 19 December 2001, by Regulation (EC) No 1882/2003 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 29 September 2003 and by Regulation (EC) No 1893/2006 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 20 December 2006;By way of an additional concession granted to the undertaking in the Walloon Region for 5 March 2021 which owned an establishment unit referred to in Article I.5, 16°, of Book I of the Code of Economic Law and whose activity is covered by one of the NACE BEL codes listed in one of the sectors or sub-sectors referred to in the following sub-classes: 1° 56.101 of the NACE BEL code; 2° 56.102 of the NACE BEL code; 3° 56.210 of the NACE BEL code; 4° 56.301 of the NACE BEL code; 5° 56.302 of the NACE BEL code; 6° 56.3.9 of the NACE BEL code. The additional concession referred to in the first paragraph shall be: 1° 4.000 euro if the number of employees is equal to 0 and less than 5; 3° 9.500 euro if the number of employees is equal to or less than 10; 4° 12,000 euro if the amount referred to in the first paragraph is equal to or higher than 10.Article 8. The decision on admissibility, payment, control and recovery of the additional allowances shall be taken by any member of the staff of level A as defined in the Walloon Government's decision of 18 December 2003 on the basis of the Walloon Code of Civil Service, designated by the Director-General of Administration.
