Royal Decree extending certain measures taken by Laws of 20 December 2020 concerning various temporary and structural provisions relating to justice in the context of the fight against the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19, of 30 April 2020 laying down various provisions relating to justice and notarial matters in the context of the fight against the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19 and of 20 May 2020 laying down various provisions relating to justice in the context of the fight against the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19 REPORT TO THE KONING Sire, The draft Royal Decree which I have the honour to submit to Your Majesty for signature, aims to extend Article 7, first paragraph of the Law of 30 April 2020, Article 4, fourth paragraph, of the Law of 20 May 2020 and a number of temporary measures taken by Law of 20 December 2020 concerning various temporary and structural provisions relating to justice in the context of the fight against the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19.§§ 5 and 6, 61quinquies, § 4 and 5, and 61sexies, § 4 of the Code of Criminal Procedure; - Law of 17 May 2006 concerning the external legal status of the sentenced persons to a custodial sentence and the rights granted to the victim in the context of the rules of criminal execution; - Law of 5 May 2014 concerning the internment (meeting of the Chamber for the protection of society); - Temporary relaxation of the requirements for the identification of signatories of authentic instruments exercising a judicial office or a post at the Constitutional Court; - Temporary extension of the scope of the e-Deposit system to any instrument of judicial entry or provision and any petition or request to the judge, and its annexes; - Extension of the time limits for the submission of documentary evidence in the context of the legal second-line assistance; - Extension of the time limits for the prosecutor of the King's opinions in the context of dummy situations and nationality; - Measures in support of the sanitary crisis in the prison referred to in Articles 59 to 62.Having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 7 March 2021; having regard to the agreement of the Secretary of State for Budget, given on 10 March 2021; having regard to the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, Article 3, § 1; having regard to the urgent need; whereas this draft relates to an extraordinary crisis situation, namely the fight against the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19 currently prevailing in Belgium, which is causing particular and serious problems in terms of public health; whereas it is of vital importance for public health and for the avoidance of a reflapping of the COVID-19 pandemic, that the necessary measures against the spread of the virus are being extended;Article 69 of the same Act shall be replaced by the following: "Art. 69. § 1. Articles 59 to 62 shall apply until 30 June 2021. § 2. Articles 63 to 68 shall apply until 15 July 2021." Article 5. This Decision shall enter into force on the day of its publication in the Belgian Official Journal. Article 6. The Minister responsible for Justice shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision.
