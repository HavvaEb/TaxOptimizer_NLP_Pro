Formal requirement(s) The following formal requirement(s) is fulfilled: - the inspection of Finance has given its opinion on 15 July 2020. - the Flemish Minister for Finance and Budget has given his agreement on 3 September 2020. - for reasons of urgency, no opinion is requested from the Council of State.
