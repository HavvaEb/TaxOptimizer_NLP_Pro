Decree approving the decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 17 du 28 May 2020 on support for meeting and receiving centres in the context of the health crisis COVID-19 The Parliament of the French Community has adopted and We, Government, confirm the following: Sole Article. The decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 17 du 28 May 2020 on support for meeting and reception centres in the context of the health crisis COVID-19, is confirmed in accordance with Article 4 of the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis of COVID-19 Announce this decree, ordering it to be published in the Belgian Official Journal.
