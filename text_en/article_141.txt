Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 48 laying down various provisions on education for social promotion in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis The Government of the French Community, having regard to the Decree of 14 November 2020 granting special powers to the Government to respond to the second wave of the COVID-19 health crisis, Article 1 (1) (f) and (h); Having regard to the Decree of 16 April 1991 on education for social promotion; Having regard to the Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 9 of 7 May 2020 laying down various provisions on education for social promotion in the framework of the COVID-19 health crisis; Having regard to the Opinion of the Council of State, given on 20 January 2021, pursuant to Article 84, paragraph 1, 3°, of the Laws to the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973;(a) The following amendments to the Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 9 of 7 May 2020 laying down various provisions on social promotion in the framework of the COFID-19 health crisis, confirmed by the Decree of 9 December 2020 shall be made:The Minister for Social Promotion is responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 4 February 2021. The Minister-President, P.-Y. JEHOLET The Minister for Higher Education, Social Promotion Education, University Hospitals, Assistance to the Youth, Justice Houses, Youth, Sport and Promotion of Brussels, V. GLATIGNY will enter into force on the day of its publication in the Belgian Official Journal, with the exception of Article 1 which will have effect from 31 December 2020.
