Having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 11 March 2021 on the granting of a specific concession to hotels and similar accommodations in the context of the crisis in relation to the Coronavirus COVID-19 The Minister of Economy, having regard to the Decree of 11 March 2004 on regional incentives to small and medium-sized enterprises, and in particular Articles 10 and 19 thereof; having regard to the Decree of 11 March 2004 on regional incentives to large enterprises, and in particular Articles 7 and 15 thereof; having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 11 March 2021 on the granting of a specific concession in favour of hotels and similar accommodations in the context of the crisis in connection with the Coronavirus COVID-19, and in particular Articles 4, 5, first and second paragraphs, and 6 thereof,Having regard to the communication from the Commission of 19 March 2020 concerning the temporary framework of State aid measures to support the economy in the current context of the widespread COVID-19 epidemic, paragraph 22; whereas the Government intends to restrict the granting of the specific concession to hotels and similar accommodation; whereas the authorisation granted under Article 5 (1) of the Walloon Government Decision of 11 March 2021 concerning the granting of a specific concession to hotels and similar accommodations in the context of the crisis relating to the coronavirus COVID-19; whereas for hotel facilities in accordance with Article 225 of the Walloon Government Decision on the Walloon Tourism Code, one of the conditions is that the building has at least six chambers reserved for customers; whereas this condition is also required to qualify for the specific concession, Decision: Article 1.the date of application for the fire safety attestation referred to in the Walloon Tourism Code. For the establishments referred to in paragraph 2 2°, the Administration shall, where appropriate, request the undertaking to send additional information to a standard attestation available on the https://indemnitecovid.wallonie.be website and to be signed by the competent municipality. Article 4. This Decision shall enter into force on the date of its signature. Names, 22 April 2021. W. BORSUS
