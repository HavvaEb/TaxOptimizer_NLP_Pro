The decision of the Flemish Government to extend to the first year of application of the provisional framework COVID-19 and to abolish the Flemish Government's decision of 3 July 2020 on the strategic transformation aid for COVID-19 Legal basis This decision is based on: - the Decree of 16 March 2012 on economic support policy, Article 35. Formal requirements The following formal requirement is fulfilled: - The Flemish Minister, responsible for the budget, has given his agreement on 10 December 2021; - No opinion has been requested from the Council of State, pursuant to Article 3 (1) of the laws on the State Council, coordinated on 12 January 1973. There is an urgent necessity because the decision of the Flemish Government to grant the aid referred to in paragraph 22 (d) of the European Temporary Framework COVID-19, under which the Flemish Protection Mechanism of State aid was notified to the European Commission, is extended until 30 June 2022 by the European Commission's communication of 18 November 2021 in paragraph 33.The decision to grant the aid must be taken by 31 December 2021 at the latest.'
