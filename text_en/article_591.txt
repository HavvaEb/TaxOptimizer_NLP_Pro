Law amending the ordinary law of 16 July 1993 supplementing the federal state structure and the law of 21 December 2009 on the system of excise duty on non-alcoholic beverages and coffee (1) FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. The Chamber of Representatives has adopted and We confirm, as follows: Article 1. This law regulates a matter referred to in Article 74 of the Constitution. Article 2. In Article 370 of the ordinary law of 16 July 1993 supplementing the federal state structure, amended by the law of 7 March 1996, the provision under 8° shall be replaced as follows: "8° unwisted fruit juices (including grape must) and unwisted alcohol, with or without added sugar or other sweeteners of the CN code 2009, with the exception of freshly pressed fruit juices and horse mackerel which have not undergone any processing on the spot and which are offered for immediate sale for consumption and which are therefore not intended for resale.'
