Decree ratifying the decisions of the Walloon Government of special powers taken in the context of the management of the health crisis during the second wave COVID-19 for the matters referred to in Article 138 of the Constitution (1) The Walloon Parliament has adopted and We , Walloon Government, ratify the following: CHAPTER I. - Overarching provision Article 1. In accordance with Article 138 of the Constitution, this Decree regulates matters referred to in Articles 127, § 1, and 128, § 1, of the Constitution. CHAPTER II. - Training and "IFAPME" Art. 2. In accordance with Article 4, § 1, of the Decree of 29 October 2020 conferring special powers on the Walloon Government to respond to the second wave of the COVID-19 health crisis for the matters governed by Article 138 of the Constitution, it is Decree of the Walloon Government of Special Powers No. 59 of 1 December 2020 on various provisions concerning vocational training adopted within the framework of the "plan rebond COVID-19", ratified under Article 138 of the Constitution. art. 3. In accordance with Article 4, § 1, of the Decree of 29 October 2020 conferring special powers on the Walloon Government to respond to the second wave of the COVID-19 health crisis for the matters governed by Article 138 of the Constitution, the Decree of the Walloon Government of Special Powers No. 70 of 18 December 2020 containing various provisions concerning training workers and subsidizing the training activities of the training centers of the IFAPME network. art. 4. In accordance with Article 4, § 1, of the Decree of 29 October 2020 conferring special powers on the Walloon Government to respond to the second wave of the COVID-19 health crisis for the matters regulated by Article 138 of the Constitution, the Decree of the Walloon Government of Special Powers No. 71 of December 18, 2020 on various provisions concerning the organization of the ongoing evaluation, exams and courses of the IFAPME network due to COVID-19. CHAPTER III. - Health and Social Action Art. 5. In accordance with Article 4, § 1, of the Decree of 29 October 2020 conferring special powers on the Walloon Government to respond to the second wave of the COVID-19 health crisis for the matters regulated by Article 138 of the Constitution, the Decree of the Walloon Government of Special Powers No. 60 of December 3, 2020 concerning various provisions on the financing of operators in the health and disabled sector in the context of the management of the COVID-19 health crisis. art. 6. In accordance with Article 4, § 1, of the Decree of 29 October 2020 conferring special powers on the Walloon Government to respond to the second wave of the COVID-19 health crisis for the matters governed by Article 138 of the Constitution, it is Decree of the Walloon Government of Special Powers No. 63 of 10 December 2020 concerning the exemption from social action subsidies and certain rules concerning the integration process of newcomers ("SPW" Internal Affairs and Social Action) has been ratified. art. 7. In accordance with Article 4, § 1, of the Decree of 29 October 2020 conferring special powers on the Walloon Government to respond to the second wave of the COVID-19 health crisis for the matters governed by Article 138 of the Constitution, it is Decree of the Walloon Government of Special Powers No. 68 of 16 December 2020 inserting an Article 47/17bis into the Walloon Code of Social Action and Health regarding the vaccination of adults against COVID-19 has been ratified. Promulgate this decree, order that it be published in the Belgian Official Gazette. Namur, 14 May 2021. The Prime Minister, E.
