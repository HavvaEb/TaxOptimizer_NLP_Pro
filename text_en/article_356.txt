Article 3. In Article 7, paragraph 4 of the same Decision, the number "35.000" shall be replaced by the number "75.000." Article 4. In Article 8, paragraph 1, of the same Decision, the following amendments shall be made: 1° the number "18" shall be replaced by the number "36"; 2° the number "6" shall be replaced by the number "24." Article 5.
