The list of successful candidates, without ranking, consists of the candidates most in line with the job description and the competence profile. This list is sent by the DG Recruitment and Development to the Chairman of the Management Committee of the FPS Public Health, Food Chain Safety and Environment. In addition to this list of laureates, a special list is drawn up (which remains valid for 4 years) of persons with a disability who have succeeded.
