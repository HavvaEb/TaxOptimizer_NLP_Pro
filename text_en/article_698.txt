Ministerial Decision amending the Ministerial Decision of 3 December 2020 concerning several provisions concerning the financing of operators in the sector of health and social action in the context of the management of the health crisis COVID-19 The Minister of Health and Social Action, Having regard to the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting the special powers to the Walloon Government in the context of the health crisis COVID-19 for matters governed by Article 138 of the Constitution, and in particular Article 2 (1) thereof; having regard to the Decree of 29 October 2020 granting special powers to the Walloon Government in response to the second wave of the health crisis COVID-19 for matters governed by Article 138 of the Constitution, Article 2 (1); having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of special powers No 14 of 10 April 2020 concerning the financing of operators in the health sector, in particular Articles 3 (1), 5 (1), 6 (1), 7 (1), 7 (1), 12 (a) (1), 13 (1) and 22 thereof, as inserted or amended by the decision of the Walloon Government of special powers of special powers in respect of persons No 14 of 10 April 2020 concerningHaving regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of special powers No 53 of 16 June 2020 concerning the various measures taken in the context of the phasing-out measures COVID-19 for the sectors of health, disability and social activities, and in particular Articles 4 (1), 5 (1), 6 (1) and 7 (1) thereof, as amended by the decision of the Walloon Government of special powers No 60 of 3 December 2020 concerning various provisions concerning the financing of operators in the health and disability sector in the context of the management of the health crisis COVID-19, and in accordance with Article 11 of the Decree of 3 December 2020 approving the decisions of the Walloon Government of special powers in the context of the management of the health crisis COVID-19 for the matters referred to in Article 138 of the Constitution,Having regard to the decision of the Walloon Government of 18 March 2020 approving the support given to the sectors of health, social action and integration into society and working life; having regard to the Ministerial Decision of 28 October 2020 laying down urgent measures to limit the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19, as amended by the Ministerial Decision of 1 November 2020; having regard to the development of the health crisis COVID-19 and the measures taken by the various levels of administration, and in particular the measures taken at the end of the consultation committee of 30 October 2020, in order to limit its spread;
