The Ministerial Decree implementing the Royal Decree of 3 July 2020 implementing Articles 12, 13, 29, 30, 44, 45, 58 and 59 of the Law of 20 December 2019 transposing Council Directive (EU) 2018/822 of 25 May 2018 amending Directive 2011/16/EU as regards mandatory automatic exchange of information in the field of taxation with regard to mandatory cross-border constructions The Vice-First Minister and Minister of Finance responsible for combating tax fraud Takes into account the Income Tax Code 1992, Articles 326/10 and 326/11, inserted by Articles 12 and 13 of the Law of 20 December 2019 transposing Council Directive (EU) 2018/822 of 25 May 2018 amending Directive 2011/16/EU as regards mandatory automatic exchange of information in the field of taxation with regard to mandatory cross-border constructions; having regard to the Code of registration, mortgage and registry duties, Articles 289a/10 and 289a/11, inserted by Articles 29 and 30 of that Act of 20 December 2019;amendment of Directive 2011/16/EU as regards mandatory automatic exchange of information in the field of taxation in relation to obliged cross-border arrangements, the management official of the general administration shall be responsible for the establishment of income taxes. Article 2. This Decision shall enter into force on 1 July 2020. Brussels, 7 July 2020. A. DE CROO
