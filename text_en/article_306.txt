Decision of the Brussels Capital Government implementing Articles 24 to 26 of the Order of 21 December 2012 determining the tax procedure in the Brussels Capital Region concerning the persons referred to in Article 27/1 of the same Order The Brussels Capital Government, having regard to the order of 21 December 2012 establishing the fiscal procedure in the Brussels Capital Region, Article 24, first and second paragraphs, as amended by the order of 7 December 2017, Articles 25 and 26, third to fifth paragraphs, as amended by the decrees of 18 December 2015 and 7 December 2017; Having regard to the Council of State opinion 66.961/4 given on 19 February 2020, pursuant to Article 84 (1) (2) of the Laws of 4 October 2018 introducing the equal opportunities test of 12 January 1973; having regard to opinion No 24/2020 of the Data Protection Authority, given on 13 March 2020;In the absence of the accounting officer responsible for tax matters, the power shall be exercised in the first paragraph by the accounting officer responsible for tax matters. Art. 4. - The transmission of the message shall be effected in one of the following ways:(b) description: size, tonnage, engine; (c) registration number on the Belgian vessel register; (c) type of instrument to be drawn up: alienation, mortgage or mortgage on acquisition. Article 5. - The transmission of the notification § 1. The transmission of the notification shall be made in one of the following ways: 1° by means of a procedure using computer techniques; or 2° by means of a registered letter. If the notification is made on the basis of the date of transmission in paragraph 1 1°, the date of transmission of the notification shall be understood as the date of receipt of the notification.The following information shall be given in relation to the dispatch of the same information by means of a procedure by which the information is sent:- in the case of a natural person: the name of the person referred to in Article 8 or, in the absence of such an identification number, the name and first names and the full address of the place of residence; - in the case of a legal person: the identification number referred to in Article 8 or, in the absence of such identification number, the name, legal form, and the full address of the registered office; - in the case of a person referred to in Article 27/1 of the order;For the purposes of this Regulation, the following definitions shall apply to the persons referred to in Article 3 of Regulation (EU) No 910/2014 of 23 July 2014 concerning electronic transactions in the internal market and repealing Directive 1999/93/EC:For the person referred to in Article 27/1 of the Ordinance, this period may in any event not exceed 30 years from the date of the performance of the authentic instrument. Article 11. - Safeguards of origin, integrity and identical content of the messages, notifications and information The origin and integrity of the content of the notices, notifications and information shall, in the case of transmission by means of a procedure involving the use of computer techniques, be ensured by means of adapted security techniques. The information contained in the notices, notifications and information shall be the same, irrespective of whether they are communicated by means of a procedure using computer techniques or by registered letter. Article 12. - Entry into force This decision shall enter into force at the time of the entry into force of Articles 24 to 26 of Article 13.
