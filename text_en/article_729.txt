Decision of the United College of the Joint Community Committee amending the Decision of the United College of the Joint Community Committee of 19 December 2019 determining the nomenclature of mobility aids to the United College of the Joint Community Committee, on the basis of the law on compulsory medical care and benefits, coordinated on 14 July 1994, Article 35, Paragraph 1, paragraph 5, and Paragraph 2, paragraph 1, paragraph 1, as amended by the Royal Decree of 25 April 1997, and Article 207a, inserted by the Law of 20 December 1995; Having regard to the order of 25 April 2019 laying down various provisions relating to Health, Assistance to Persons and Family Assistance, Article 3; Having regard to the Royal Decree of 8 December 1997 laying down the detailed rules for the indexation of the performance of compulsory medical care insurance scheme; Having regard to the decision of the United College of the Joint Community Commission of 19 December 2019 establishing the nomenclature of mobility aids; having regard to the opinion of the Board of Health and Assistance to Persons of Iriscare, given on 13 July 2021, to the proposal of the Commission of theArticle 2 of the Act of 1 March 1977 establishing a system linking certain expenditure in the public sector to the index of national consumer prices.' Article 2 of the same Decision replaces the Annex by the Annex to this Decision. Article 3 of the Royal Decree of 8 December 1997 laying down the detailed rules for the indexation of benefits in the compulsory medical insurance scheme, as amended by the Royal Decrees of 12 December 2001 and 29 January 2002, is hereby repealed with regard to mobility aids. Article 4. This Decision has effect with effect from 1 January 2022. Art. 5. The Members of the United College, responsible for Welfare and Health, are responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 16 December 2021.
