Royal Decree amending the Royal Decree of 13 December 2020 implementing Article 3 (2) of the Law of 6 November 2020 to allow, within the framework of the Coronavirus COVID-19 epidemic, nurse activities to be carried out by persons legally not competent to do so FILIP, King of Belgians, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. Considering the Law of 6 November 2020 to allow, within the framework of the Coronavirus COVID-19 epidemic, nurse activities to be carried out by persons legally not competent to do so, Article 3 (2); having regard to the Royal Decree of 13 December 2020 implementing Article 3 (2) of the Law of 6 November 2020 to allow, within the framework of the Coronavirus COVID-19-epemic nurse activities to be carried out by persons legally not empowered to do so; having regard to the opinion of the Finance inspector, given on 22 February 2021 and 12 March 2021; having regard to the request for urgent treatment, justified by the fact that the Law of 6 November 2020 to allow, within the framework of the COVID-19-ocese activities to be exercised within the2 of the Law of 6 November 2020 to allow the coronavirus-COVID-19 epidemic nurse activities to be carried out by persons not legally authorised to do so, the words "1 April 2021" shall be replaced by the words "1 October 2021." Article 2. In the Annex to the same Decision, the words "sorry wound care" shall be inserted between the words "preparation, execution and supervision of:" and the words "sorry wounds with wicks and drains." Article 3. This Decision shall enter into force on 31 March 2021. Article 4. The Minister for Public Health shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Given in Brussels, 25 March 2021. FILIP Van Koningswege : The Minister of Health, Fr. VANDENBROUCKE
