The Decree amending Article 5 of the Decree of 29 October 2020 granting special powers to the Walloon Government to react to the second wave of the health crisis COVID-19 for matters governed by Article 138 of the Constitution (1) The Walloon Parliament has adopted and We, Walloon Government, confirm the following: Article 1. In Article 5 of the Decree of 29 October 2020 granting special powers to the Walloon Government to respond to the second wave of the health crisis COVID-19 for matters governed by Article 138 of the Constitution, the word "one" is replaced by the word "two." Article 2. This Decree enters into force on the day following its ratification by the Walloon Government. Announce this decree, recommending that it be published in the Belgian Official Journal.
