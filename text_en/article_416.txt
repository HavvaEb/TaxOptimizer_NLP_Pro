Royal Decree amending Annex III to the Royal Decree 92 on the legal benefits for temporary unemployment of FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greetings. Having regard to the Code of Income Tax 1992, Article 275, § 1; Having regard to the KB/WIB 92; Having regard to the opinion of the Finance Inspector given on 16 March 2021; Having regard to the agreement of the State Secretary for Budget of 29 March 2021; having regard to the laws of the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, Article 3 (1); having regard to the urgent need; Whereas the reduction of the percentage of the holding tax on the statutory benefits for temporary unemployment of 26,75 pct. to 15 pct. applies only to the benefits until 31 March 2021; - that this percentage of 26,75 pct. was determined to take into account a limited number of days of temporary unemployment on a monthly basis;March 2021. FILIP Van Koningswege : De Minister van Financiën, V. VAN PETEGHEM
