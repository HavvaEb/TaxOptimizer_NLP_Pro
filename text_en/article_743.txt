Decision of the Walloon Government on the granting of an additional concession to the establishments closed by decision in the context of the crisis in connection with the Coronavirus COVID-19 The Walloon Government, having regard to the Decree of 11 March 2004 on regional incentives for small and medium-sized enterprises, and in particular Articles 10 and 19 thereof; having regard to the report of 4 February 2021 drawn up in accordance with Article 3 2° of the Decree of 11 April 2014 implementing the resolutions of the United Nations Women's Conference held in Beijing in September 1995 and integrating the gender dimension into the whole of regional policies; having regard to the opinion of the Inspector of Finance, given on 1 February 2021; having regard to the assent of the Minister of Budget, given on 4 February 2021; having regard to the request for an opinion within a period of 5 days, addressed to the Council of State on 9 February 2021, pursuant to Article 84 (1) of the Laws of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973;Regulation (EEC) No 3037/90 of the Council of 9 October 1990 on the statistical nomenclature of economic activities in the European Community, as amended by Commission Regulation (EEC) No 762/93 of 24 March 1993, by Regulation (EC) No 290/2002 of 19 December 2001 on the application of the principle of equal treatment for men and women in matters of social security to employed persons and to members of their families moving within the meaning of Article 1 of Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 20 September 2000 on the application of social security schemes to employed persons and their families moving within the meaning of Article 1 of Regulation (EEC) No 1408/71 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 20 December 2000 on the application of social security schemes to employed persons and their families moving within the meaning of Article 1 of Council Regulation (EEC) No 574/72 of 9 October 1990 on the classification of economic activities in the European Community, as amended by Regulation (EEC) No 76/93 of the Commission of 24 March 1993,The Minister shall, in accordance with the detailed rules laid down by him, grant an additional concession to the undertaking which, before 19 October 2020 in the Walloon Region, owned an establishment unit referred to in Article I.2, 16° of Book I of the Economic Law Code and whose activity is covered by one of the NACE BEL codes listed in one of the sectors or sub-sectors referred to in the following sub-classes: 1° 56.101 of NACE BEL code; 2° 56.102 of NACE BEL code; 3° 56.301 of NACE BEL code; 4° 56.302 of NACE BEL code; 5° 56.309 of NACE BEL code; 6° 93.110 of NACE BEL code; 7° 93.121 to 93.129 of NACE BEL code; 8° 93.130 of NACE BEL code; 9° 93.199 of NACE BEL code; 10° 93.211 of NACE BEL code;The NACE-BEL code shall be the same as the NACE-BEL code; 6° 82.300 of the NACE-BEL code; 7° 85.510 of the NACE-BEL code; 8° 85.520 of the NACE-BEL code; 9° 85.532 of the NACE-BEL code; 10° 90.021 of the NACE-BEL code; 11° 90.041 of the NACE-BEL code; 12° 90.042 of the NACE-BEL code; 13° 91.030 of the NACE-BEL code; 14° 91.041 of the NACE-BEL code; 15° 92 of NACE-BEL code in respect of the casinos, games halls and wagers; 16° 93.292 of the NACE-BEL code; 17° 93.299 of the NACE-BEL code; 18° 96.02.1 of the NACE-BEL code; 19° 96.40 of the NACE-BEL code; 21° NACE-BEL code in respect of the NACE-BEL code.Article 6 Within the time limits laid down by the Minister, the undertaking shall, by means of a form on the web platform, submit its application for additional concession to the Administration. When submitting the file on the web platform, in particular the following information shall be provided by the undertaking: 1° its number from the Cross Point Bank of Enterprises; 2° the NACE BEL code for the activity for which the undertaking applies for additional concession; 3° a statement of honour to be entered on the web platform; 4° the account number of the undertaking; in particular, the undertaking shall declare, by means of the declaration referred to in paragraph 2, 3°, that it does not exceed the ceilings laid down in Regulation 1407/2013, that it shall be EUR 200,000 for the last three tax years and that it shall be covered by one of the NACE BEL codes referred to in Article 3 (1) and Article 4 (1).Namur, February 19, 2021.
