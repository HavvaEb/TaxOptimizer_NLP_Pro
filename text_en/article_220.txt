Decree approving the decision of special powers No 39 of the Government of the French Community of 20 June 2020 seeking to resume film recordings by guaranteeing the risks associated with the COVID-19 crisis The Parliament of the French Community has adopted and We, Government, confirm what follows: Sole Article. The decision of special powers No 39 of the Government of the French Community of 20 June 2020 seeking to resume film recordings by guaranteeing the risks associated with the COVID-19 crisis is confirmed with effect from the date of its entry into force in accordance with Article 4 of the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis relating to the Covid-19 coronavirus.
