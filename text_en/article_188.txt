Decision of the Flemish Government granting an optional subsidy to the airport operating company Ostend-Brugge in the framework of COVID-19 for the financial year 2020 Legal basis This decision is based on: - the Law of 16 May 2003 laying down the general provisions applicable to the budgets, the control of grants and the accounts of the communities and the regions, as well as on the organisation of the audit by the Court of Auditors; - the Decree of 26 June 2020 adjusting the general budget of the Flemish Community for the financial year 2020; - the Decision of the Flemish Government of 2 October 2019 determining the powers of the members of the Flemish Government; - the Flemish Codex Public Finances of 29 March 2019; - the Decision of the Flemish Codex Public Finance of 17 May 2019.2020. This grant will be paid on account number BE36 7310 3623 0481 of the airport operating company Ostend-Brugge. Art. 3. The Flemish Minister responsible for Mobility and Public Works is responsible for the implementation of this decision. Brussels, 10 July 2020. The Prime Minister of the Flemish Government, J.
