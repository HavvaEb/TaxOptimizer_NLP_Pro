Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 12 derogating for the year 2020 from the Decree of 18 January 2018 establishing a register of grants in the French Community in the context of the health crisis of COVID-19 The Government of the French Community, having regard to the Decree of 18 January 2018 establishing a register of grants in the French Community; having regard to the Decree of 17 March 2020 conferring special powers on the Government in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis, Article 1 (1) (g); whereas, in accordance with Article 1 (1) (g) of the Decree of 17 March 2020 conferring special powers on the Government in the context of the COVID-19 health crisis, any situation which raises a problem within the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic and its consequences must be prevented and treated as a matter of serious danger; whereas the decision of the Minister of Security and Home Affairs of 23 March 2020, amended on 17 April 2020, on emergency measures to restrict the distribution of the coronavirus COVID-19 companies,Finally, that these derogations are not intended to establish a permanent rule which would allow these deadlines to be extended for the following years and to be limited to what is strictly necessary; to the nomination of the Minister of Budget; after deliberation, Decision: Article 1. By way of derogation from Article 2 of the Decree of 18 January 2018 establishing a register of grants in the French Community, the Government shall, by 15 August 2020 at the latest, submit a report containing all grants granted between 1 January and 31 December 2019 by the subsidising bodies under the jurisdiction of the French Community. Taking into account the evolution of the crisis, may decide to extend this period of communication by one month.
