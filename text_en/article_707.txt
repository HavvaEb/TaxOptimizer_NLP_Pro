Royal Decree granting a dotation to the RIZIV for the year 2021 in the context of the fight against the COVID-19 virus FILIP, King of Belgium, to all who are now and will be listed below, Our Greet. Having regard to the Law of 22 May 2003 on the organisation of the budget and on the compatibility of the federal State, Articles 121 to 124; having regard to the Law of 22 December 2020 on the general budget for the financial year 2021, Article 2.06.2; having regard to the Law of 2 April 2021 on the second adjustment of the general budget for expenditure for the financial year 2021; having regard to the Royal Decree of 16 November 1994 on administrative and budgetary control; having regard to the Royal Decree No 20 of 13 May 2020 on temporary measures to combat the COVID-19 pandemic and to ensure continuity of medical care; having regard to the Royal Decree No 33 of 23 June 2020 on the adjustment of the revenue of medical care insurance to cover for specific expenses to the COVID-19 pandemic;The Belgian Official Journal is published Art. 5. The Minister responsible for Social Affairs and the Minister responsible for Self-employed Persons, are each responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Given in Brussels, 27 June 2021.
