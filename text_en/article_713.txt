Special Powers Decision No 2020/007 of the United College of the Joint Community Committee on the temporary assignment to the Bi-Community Health Service, Assistance to Persons and Family Assistance in the context of the COOVID-19 Health Crisis REPORT TO THE UNITED MEETING OF THE COMMON COMMUNITY COMMISSION The Special Powers Decision No 2020/007 responds to a need for an efficient management of the COOVID-19 Health Crisis. The Special Powers Decision amends the Order of 23 March 2017 on the establishment of the Bi-Community Health Service, Assistance to Persons and Family Assistance to the following point: In the context of the prevention policy of the GGC and during the health crisis, Iriscare is charged with the task of purchasing protective material for the purpose of this mission.
