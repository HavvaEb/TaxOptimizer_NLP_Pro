Decision of the Government of the French Community extending the measures taken in the Decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 concerning various provisions relating to civil service within the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic The Government of the French Community, having regard to the special law of 8 August 1980 on the reform of the institutions, replacing Article 87 (3) by the special law of 8 August 1988 and amended by the special law of 6 January 2014; Having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 22 July 1996 laying down the Staff Regulations of officials of the Services of the Government of the French Community; having regard to the decision of the Government of the French Community of 2 June 2004 on the privileges and absences of staff of the Government of the French Community, the High Council for the Audiovisual Sector and the institutions of the Sector XVII Committee,The effect of Articles 1, 2, 3 and 4 of the Decision of the Government of the French Community of 7 May 2020 concerning various provisions relating to civil service in the framework of the COVID-19 pandemic shall be extended until 30 June 2020. This Decision shall take effect from 18 May 2020. Article 3. The Minister of Civil Service shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 28 May 2020. Because of the Government of the French Community: The Minister-President, P.-Y. JEHOLET The Minister of Budget, Civil Service, Equal Opportunities and in charge of the supervision of Wallonie Bruxelles Enseignement', Fr.
