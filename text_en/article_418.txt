For the purposes of this Decision, the following shall mean: 1° 'decision regulating the procedure and the signature of the Government's instruments': the decision of the Brussels Capital Government of 18 July 2000 on the rules of procedure and on the arrangements for the signature of the Government's acts; 2° 'powers in respect of public works, supplies and services': the powers delegated to the Ministers indicated in the Decision of the Brussels Capital Government determining the distribution of powers between the Ministers of the Brussels Capital Government on the basis of Article 5, 10° of the decision determining the procedure and the signature of the Government's instruments;- the signing of all correspondence relating to the award, award and execution of contracts; - the placing of orders and the signing of order forms in the case of framework agreements; - the commitment and settlement of appropriations. Article 3. All decisions taken on the basis of this decision shall be deemed to have been taken by the leading officials and all documents shall be signed jointly by the two leading officials. Article 4. This decision shall be carried out under the joint responsibility of the Prime Minister and the Minister of Finance, Budget, Public Administration, Promotion of Multilingualism and of the Imago of Brussels. Article 5. The executive officials shall report on the implementation of this decision to the Minister-President and the Minister of Finance, Budget, Public Office, Promotion of Multilingualism and of the Imago of Brussels, who shall report on a monthly basis to the Government.
