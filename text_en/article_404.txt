Decision approving the Cooperation Agreement of 25 February 2021 amending the Framework Agreement for cooperation of 27 February 2014 between the French Community, the Walloon Region and the French Community Commission concerning inter-French consultations on health and assistance to persons and on common principles applicable to them to the latter to deal with the COVID-19 emergencies The Parliament of the French Community has adopted and We, Government, confirm the following: Single Article - In accordance with the Cooperation Agreement of 25 February 2021 amending the Framework Agreement for cooperation of 27 February 2014 between the French Community, the Walloon Region and the French Community Commission on inter-French consultations on health and assistance to persons and on common principles applicable to the latter to the latter to the COVID-19 emergency aid scheme.Having regard to the Decree of the Assembly of the French Community Commission of 4 April 2014 concerning the powers of the French Community, the exercise of which is transferred to the Walloon Region and the French Community Commission, Article 13; having regard to the Decree of the Walloon Region of 11 April 2014 concerning the powers of the French Community, the exercise of which is transferred to the Walloon Region and the French Community Commission, Article 13; whereas solutions must be found to deal with the emergency situations arising from the COVID-19 pandemic and that the framework agreement for cooperation of 27 February 2014 between the French Community, the Walloon Region and the French Community Commission concerning the consultations between French speakers on health and assistance to persons and on the common principles applicable to these matters must be amended without delay, in order to consult the Ministerial Committee and the Conciliation Body within the framework of the adoption of law or regulations specifically aimed at combating COVID-19; whereas the management of the pandemic activity of theIn Chapter V of the Framework Agreement for cooperation of 27 February 2014 between the French Community, the Walloon Region and the French Community Commission on the consultations between French speakers on health and assistance to persons and on the common principles applicable to these matters, a Section 2/1 shall be inserted as follows: "The procedure for extreme emergency situations in the context of the fight against the COVID-19 pandemic'. Article 2. In Section 2/1, inserted by Article 1, an Article 15/1 shall be inserted as follows: "Art. 15/1. If the preliminary draft, the decree or draft regulatory decision which is submitted for consultation is intended specifically to combat COVID-19, the Ministerial Committee and the consultative body shall be consulted simultaneously and shall have a maximum period of two calendar days to deliver an opinion. If within the time limit referred to in the preceding paragraph, the formality shall be deemed to be fulfilled.
