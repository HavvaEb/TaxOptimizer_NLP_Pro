This Decision is based on: - the Decree of 12 February 2021 to take urgent temporary measures in education following the corona crisis (VI), Article 3, 4, 7, 9, 17, 19 and 20; - the Decree of 15 June 2007 on adult education, Article 91; - the Decree of 30 October 2020 to take urgent and temporary measures in education in times of corona crisis (IV) and amending the Decree of basic education of 25 February 1997 and the Codex secondary education of 17 December 2010 on the external quality control of equal educational opportunities policy, Article 16; The following formal requirements have been fulfilled: - the Inspection of Finance has given its opinion on 26 March 2021 and 5 May 2021 on the basis of the Government's decision of 6 May 2021 on the basis of the State's decision to extend the period of validity of various measures pursuant to COVD-19.This decision is based on the following motives: - The COOVID-19 pandemic will continue for a long time during the school year 2020-2021 and this has a major impact on the schools, the academies of part-time art education, the basic education centres, the adult education centres, the CLBs and the internates. In order to continue the provision of education in safe conditions and to guarantee the right to learn for the pupils, measures already taken should be urgently extended from 1 January 2021 to 30 June and 31 August 2021 respectively; - In the context of the outbreak of COVID-19, these educational institutions should continue to incur a lot of additional costs for the purchase of material for hygiene and safety materials, such as mouth masks, cleansing products and other necessary hygienic infrastructure.For the period from 1 January 2021 to 30 June 2021, additional resources shall be granted for the period from 1 January 2021 to 30 June 2021 for additional expenditure against the dissemination of COVID-19. § 3. The amount per institution referred to in paragraphs 1, 1°, 2°, and 3° shall be determined by multiplying the number of financially viable internals on the counting day or corresponding counting period by EUR 13.80. By way of derogation from this provision: 1° the amount per institution shall be indicated in Article III.1, paragraph 1, paragraph 1, paragraph 1, of the codification of certain provisions for the education of 28 October 2016, plus the amount fixed on the basis of the average number of internals of September 2020 used for the determination of the additional staff framework for the opening and operation of school-free days; 2° the amount for the institution referred to in Article III.20, of the same codification, plus the amount for the following periods of 28 October 2016, plus the amount fixed for the period from 28 October 2016 to 31 December 2020.The amount is allocated to the CLBs for additional expenditure against the dissemination of COVID-19. The amount is allocated to the CLBs on the basis of their share in the new framework. Art. 5. Within the authorisation given to the Flemish Government in Article 91 of the Decree of 15 June 2007 on adult education, an additional amount of 192.233.20 euros is allocated to the basic education centres in order to compensate for the additional expenditure against the spread of COVID-19 from 1 January 2021 to 31 August 2021. These additional funds are allocated to the centres for basic education according to the proportion of weighted funding points for the framework each centre reached in the reference year 2019.
