The Minister of Justice, having regard to the Law of 20 December 2020 laying down various temporary and structural provisions on justice in the context of the fight against the spread of COVID-19, Article 66 (2); having regard to the Royal Decree of 29 March 2021 renewing certain measures taken by Laws of 20 December 2020 laying down various temporary and structural provisions on justice in the context of the fight against the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19, of 30 April 2020 laying down various provisions on justice and notarial in the context of the fight against the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19 and of 20 May 2020 laying down various provisions on justice in the context of the fight against the spread of the coronavirus COVID-19; having regard to the Laws of State, coordinated by the Council of State, of 12 January 1973,This Decision shall enter into force on 1 April 2021. Brussels, 29 March 2021. V. VAN QUICKENBORNE.
