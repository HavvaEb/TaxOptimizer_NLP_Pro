The Decree approving the Cooperation Agreement of 25 February 2021 amending the Framework Agreement for cooperation of 27 February 2014 between the French Community, the Walloon Region and the French Community Committee on Inter-French-speaking Consultation on Health and Assistance to Persons and on the Common Principles applicable to the latter, in order to deal with the emergencies COVID-19 (1) The Walloon Parliament has adopted and We, Walloon Government, confirm the following: Article 1. This Decree governs, in accordance with Article 138 of the Constitution, matters referred to in Articles 127 and 128 of the Constitution. Art. 2. In accordance with the Cooperation Agreement of 25 February 2021 amending the Framework Agreement for cooperation of 27 February 2014 between the French Community, the Walloon Region and the French Community Commission on the Inter-French-speaking Consultation on Health and Assistance to Persons and on the Common Principles applicable to the latter, in order to deal with the emergencies COVID-19.
