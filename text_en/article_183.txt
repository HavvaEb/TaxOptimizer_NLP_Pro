Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 62 on the subsidy of the purchase of material for online education in education for social promotion in the context of the COOVID-19 health crisis The Government of the French Community, having regard to the Decree of 14 November 2020 granting to the Government of special powers to cope with the second wave of the COOVID-19 health crisis, Article 1 (1) (b), (f) and (h), having regard to the opinion of the Council of State No 68.818/2, issued on 10 February 2021, applying Article 84 (1), first paragraph, 3°, of the laws on the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973 and Article 2, second paragraph, of the Decree of 14 November 2020 granting to the Government of special powers to deal with the second wave of the COOVID-19 health crisis;The grant to be granted under the budget line "2.5.04 Social promotion" of the "Cell for Emergency Assistance and Recast" of SACA, as established by the programme decree of 9 December 2020 concerning various measures to counter the effects of the coronavirus crisis, School buildings, Budgetary Funds, Fund Ecureuil, WBE, Health, Media, Permanent Education, Study Grants, Scientific Research and Compulsory Education - Title I. Art. 2. The amount referred to in Article 1 shall be allocated to the organizing powers of education for social promotion in relation to the number of pupils regularly enrolled in 2018-2019. Art. 3. The subsidy referred to in Article 1 shall be intended to enable the beneficiaries to cover, by means of eligible expenditure, the costs incurred in connection with the purchase of material necessary for online education. This material shall be made available to the learners (computers, tablets, etc.) or the teachers to support and development of online education (camera, interactive whiteboard, green background, etc.).The application for payment of the grant must be made to the administrative services by 31 October 2021 at the latest. Article 5. In order to be eligible for reimbursement, the equipment must be ordered from the supplier between 1 March 2020 and 30 June 2021. Article 6. This decision enters into force the day on which it is signed. Article 7. The Minister responsible for social promotion education is responsible for implementing this decision. Brussels, 15 February 2021. The Prime Minister, P.-Y.
