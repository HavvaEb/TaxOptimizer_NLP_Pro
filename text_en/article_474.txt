Decree approving the decision of the Government of the French Community of Special Forces No 21 of 11 June 2020 concerning the adjusted budget of the Centre du Cinéma et de l'Audiovisuel (Centre for the Film and Audiovisual Sector) in support of the restoration of the film sector in the context of the COVID-19 crisis The Parliament of the French Community has adopted and we, the Government, confirm the following: Sole Article. The decision of the Government of the French Community of Special Forces No 21 of 11 June 2020 providing for the adjusted budget of the Centre du Cinéma et de l'Audiovisuel (Centre for the film sector and the audiovisual sector) in support of the restoration of the film sector in the context of the COVID-19 crisis is hereby confirmed pursuant to Article 4 of the Decree of 17 March 2020 conferring special powers to the Government in the context of the COVID-19 coronavirus.
