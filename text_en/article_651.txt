Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 59 on the support to the meeting and residence centres in the context of the second wave of the health crisis COVID-19 The Government of the French Community, having regard to the Decree of 14 November 2020 granting to the Government of special powers to cope with the second wave of the health crisis COVID-19, Articles 1 (1) (d) and 2; Having regard to the opinion of the Financial Inspection, issued on 15 January 2021; Having regard to the agreement of the Minister of the Budget, given on 28 January 2021; Having regard to the urgent need for action by the Council of State, delivered on 4 February 2021, in application of Article 84, paragraph 1, paragraph 3, of the Laws of the State, coordinated on 12 January 1973; Having regard to the need for rapid measures to ensure the viability of the youth sector by means of measures taken in the context of the second wave of the health crisis of COVID19;Whereas this financial aid shall also be granted in accordance with the classification of the operator in the main system for "meeting and residence centres" as referred to in Article 11 of the abovementioned Decree of 20 July 2000 and the rate of occupancy in terms of overnight stays of operators for a given period; whereas, on the proposal of the Minister of Youth; after discussion, Decision: Article 1, the purpose of this Decision is to lay down the conditions for the granting of exceptional grants to operators referred to in paragraph 2 who encounter financial difficulties as a result of the health measures which are and will be taken during the second wave of the health crisis COVID-19 and which have an effect on their activities in the field of reception of the public; whereas the operators referred to in this Decision shall be the meeting and residence centres referred to in Articles 4 and 5 of the Decree of 20 July 2000 determining the conditions for recognition and subsidy of youth centres and youth information centres and their federations.The operator shall not receive a maximum of 100 per cent of the maximum number of overnight stays laid down in paragraph 2 according to its classification; 2° if the operator receives 80 per cent of the maximum number of overnight stays laid down in paragraph 2 in accordance with its classification; 2° if the operator has an occupancy rate in terms of overnight stays ranging from 31 to 40,99 per cent, he shall receive 90 per cent of the maximum amount fixed in paragraph 2 on the basis of its classification; 3° if the operator has a occupancy rate in terms of overnight stays determined in accordance with its classification; 2° if the operator receives 80 per cent of the maximum number of overnight stays established in paragraph 2 shall receive 80 per cent of the maximum number of overnight stays determined in accordance with its classification;If the operator has received subsidies from other levels of government, these amounts shall be taken into account in the calculation of the amount of the grant, in order to avoid double subsidisation for the same purpose. § 2 Applications may only be submitted using the form established by the Government. § 3. Without prejudice to the provisions of Article 3 (4), the grant shall be paid in two instalments fixed as follows: 1° a first instalment corresponding to 80% of the grant amount shall be paid when the grant decision is adopted; 2° a second instalment corresponding to 20% of the grant amount shall be paid after verification and validation of the supporting documents referred to in paragraph 1. This decision shall enter into force on the day on which it is published in the Belgian Official Journal. Art. 6. The Minister of Youth shall be responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 11 February 2021.
