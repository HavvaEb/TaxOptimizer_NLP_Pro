Decision of the Government of the French Community of special powers No 20 adopted pursuant to the Decree of 17 March 2020 granting special powers to the Government in the context of the health crisis in connection with the COVID-19 coronavirus, approving the adjustment of the adjusted budget of the public undertaking for the New Information and Communication Technologies of the French Community for the financial year 2020 The Government of the French Community, Having regard to the Act of 16 March 1954 concerning the control of certain institutions of public utility, as amended; having regard to the Decree of 25 October 2018 concerning the "Entreprise public of the Technologies nouvelles de l'Information et de la Communication de la Communauté française (ETNIC) ',This Decision shall enter into force on the day on which it is signed.
