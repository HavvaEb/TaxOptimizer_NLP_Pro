The Flemish Government's decision to amend the Flemish Government's decision of 18 December 2020 laying down measures to continue the support of persons with disabilities in periods of flare-up of COVID-19 Legal grounds This decision is based on: - the Decree of 7 May 2004 establishing the internal self-employed agency with legal personality Flemish Agency for Persons with disabilities, Article 8 2° and 3°, as amended by the Decree of 25 April 2014, and 7°, and 12°, inserted by Decree of 25 April 2014, Article 19, replaced by the Decree of 25 April 2014, and Article 23, second paragraph, inserted by the Decree of 25 April 2014 and amended by the Decree of 8 June 2018; - the Decree of 25 April 2014 concerning the personal financing of persons with disabilities and reforming the method of financing and support for persons with disabilities, Article 11.in Article 10, paragraph 5, third paragraph, 2°, or fourth paragraph, of the abovementioned Decision." Article 2. In Article 16, second paragraph, of the same Decision, the date "31 March 2021" is replaced by the date "30 June 2021." Article 3. Article 1, 1°, has effect from 1 October 2020. Article 1, 2°, has effect from 1 January 2021. Article 2 has effect from 31 March 2021. Article 4. The Flemish Minister, responsible for persons with disabilities, is responsible for the implementation of this Decision. Brussels, 23 April 2021. The Prime Minister of the Flemish Government, JAMBON The Flemish Minister of Welfare, Public Health, Family and Armed Forces, W. BEKE
