The Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Finance, having regard to the Code of Friendly and Forced Recovery of Tax and Non-fiscal Claims, introduced by the Law of 13 April 2019, Article 15, § 1 2°; In view of the opinion of the Minister of Finance, given on 21 February 2020; whereas it is a purely implementing act of existing legislation and does not in itself have any new budgetary impact, an agreement of the Minister of Budget is therefore not required; In view of the laws on the Council of State, coordinated on 12 January 1973, Article 3, § 1; whereas the provisions of this Decision are not of a regulatory nature as required by Article 3, § 1, paragraph 1, of the same laws; In accordance with Article 6, § 2 of the Law of 15 December 2013 laying down various provisions on simplification, an impact analysis of the rules is not necessary, Decision.
